# Ministry of Health — Kemenkes

## Overview

The **Kementerian Kesehatan Republik Indonesia** (Ministry of Health, abbreviated **Kemenkes**) is the primary regulatory authority for medical devices in Indonesia. For manufacturers seeking to place a medical device on the Indonesian market, Kemenkes is the competent authority you interact with — it issues the NIE (*Nomor Izin Edar*), operates the Regalkes registration portal, and conducts market surveillance.

Kemenkes operates under the President of the Republic through the Cabinet system. The specific operational unit responsible for medical devices is the **Directorate General of Pharmaceutical and Medical Devices** (*Direktorat Jenderal Kefarmasian dan Alat Kesehatan*).

---

## Key Functions Relevant to Medical Device Manufacturers

| Function | Description |
|---|---|
| **NIE Issuance** | Issues the Nomor Izin Edar — the mandatory marketing authorisation for all medical devices |
| **Regalkes Portal** | Operates and maintains Indonesia's online device registration system |
| **Technical Evaluation** | Conducts administrative and technical review of registration dossiers |
| **IDAK Oversight** | Oversees the IDAK (distribution licence) system via OSS |
| **CDAKB Inspections** | Conducts Good Distribution Practice inspections of local distributors and LARs |
| **Market Surveillance** | "Market sampling" — physical inspection of products available in hospitals and pharmacies to verify label compliance and NIE validity |
| **Post-Market Oversight** | Receives adverse event reports, manages NIE renewals, and issues sanctions for non-compliance |

```
    (Directorate General of Pharmaceutical and Medical Devices)
    ├── Directorate of Medical Devices
    │   ├── Pre-market evaluation (NIE, registration)
    │   └── Post-market surveillance
    └── Directorate of Pharmaceutical Goods
        └── (Pharmaceutical products — separate stream)
```

## Regalkes — The Digital Interface

All medical device registrations are processed through the **Regalkes** portal (`regalkes.kemkes.go.id`). Kemenkes developed Regalkes as the single digital platform for:

- Submitting new device registrations
- Paying PNBP (non-tax government revenue) fees
- Responding to evaluator clarification requests
- Managing NIE renewals and change notifications
- Monitoring application status in real time

:::caution
Regalkes undergoes periodic scheduled maintenance shutdowns (for example, a closure was planned for December 2024). Plan submissions with buffer time around known maintenance periods.
:::

## Contacting Kemenkes

For device registration matters:

- **Regalkes portal**: [regalkes.kemkes.go.id](https://regalkes.kemkes.go.id)
- **Official Kemenkes website**: [www.kemkes.go.id](https://www.kemkes.go.id)
- Formal correspondence is typically conducted through the Regalkes system and the LAR's registered account

:::note
Kemenkes communications are conducted exclusively in Bahasa Indonesia. Engaging a local regulatory affairs professional (consultant) or LAR to manage correspondence is essential, not optional — official documents must be in Indonesian, and queries require prompt, accurate responses in Indonesian to meet regulatory deadlines.
:::

Technical review is more variable and depends on dossier completeness and complexity; expect 30-90 days for initial technical review, with clarification rounds adding 14-30 days per response cycle.
