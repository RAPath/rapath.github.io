# Useful Links

## Indonesian Government Portals

| Resource | URL | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Regalkes Portal** | [regalkes.kemkes.go.id](https://regalkes.kemkes.go.id) | Online medical device registration portal — NIE applications, renewals, change notifications |
| **Kemenkes Official Website** | [www.kemkes.go.id](https://www.kemkes.go.id) | Ministry of Health; regulations, news, announcements |
| **BPOM Official Website** | [www.pom.go.id](https://www.pom.go.id) | National Agency of Drug and Food Control; regulations, safety alerts, public notices |
| **BPOM e-Registration** | [e-reg.pom.go.id](https://e-reg.pom.go.id) | BPOM's online portal (for pharmaceutical products) |
| **OSS — Online Single Submission** | [oss.go.id](https://oss.go.id) | Business licensing portal; IDAK applications, NIB registration |
| **BPJPH — Halal** | [bpjph.halal.go.id](https://bpjph.halal.go.id) | Halal Product Assurance Agency; Halal certification information |
