# Technical Standards

Vietnam recognises international technical standards as the basis for demonstrating conformity with the essential safety and performance requirements of Decree 98/2021. These are primarily ISO and IEC standards adopted through ASEAN harmonisation.

## Essential Principles of Safety and Performance

Decree 98/2021 requires devices to conform to **Essential Principles of Safety and Performance** — a set of requirements derived from the ASEAN AMDD. Manufacturers demonstrate compliance through:
- Conformance to harmonised technical standards (where available)
- Common Specifications (where harmonised standards are absent)
- Other technically justified methods

## Key recognised standards

### Quality management
| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| ISO 13485:2016 | Quality management systems — medical devices |
| ISO 14971:2019 | Risk management for medical devices |

### Biocompatibility
| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| ISO 10993 series | Biological evaluation of medical devices |
| ISO 10993-1 | Evaluation and testing within a risk management process |

### Sterilisation
| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| ISO 11135 | Sterilisation using ethylene oxide |
| ISO 11137 series | Sterilisation using radiation |
| ISO 17665 series | Sterilisation using moist heat |
| ISO 11607 series | Packaging for terminally sterilised devices |

### Electrical safety and EMC
| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| IEC 60601-1 | Electrical safety of medical electrical equipment |
| IEC 60601-1-2 | EMC requirements for medical electrical equipment |
| IEC 60601-1-6 | Usability |
| IEC 60601-1-11 | Home healthcare environment |

### Software
| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| IEC 62304 | Medical device software — software life cycle processes |
| IEC 62366-1 | Application of usability engineering to medical devices |

### IVD
| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| ISO 18113 series | IVD labelling |
| ISO 15189 | Medical laboratories — requirements |

## Vietnam-specific national standards (TCVN)

Vietnam also has a body of national standards (TCVN — Tiêu chuẩn Việt Nam) applicable to medical devices. Where a TCVN standard duplicates an ISO/IEC standard, compliance with either is generally acceptable. Check DAV guidance for any mandatory TCVN standards in specific device categories.

:::note
The list of recognised standards in Vietnam is updated periodically. Always verify the current list with DAV or your Authorised Representative before finalising your Essential Principles checklist.
:::
