# Special Access Routes

## What are Special Access Routes?

**Special Access Routes (SARs)** allow unregistered medical devices to be supplied in Singapore under specific, limited circumstances where the normal product registration pathway is not applicable or practical. SARs are exceptions — not alternatives — to the standard registration pathway.

## Available routes

### Unregistered devices requested by a qualified practitioner
A licensed medical, dental, or other qualified practitioner may apply to import an unregistered device for a specific patient under their care. This pathway is intended for individual patient use, not for general commercial supply.

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/qualified-practitioner-request](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/qualified-practitioner-request)

### Unregistered devices requested by a licensed healthcare facility
A licensed healthcare institution (hospital, clinic, etc.) may apply to import unregistered devices for use within that institution for patient care purposes.

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/phmc-request](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/phmc-request)

### Import for re-export of unregistered devices
Allows unregistered devices to be imported into Singapore solely for the purpose of re-export to another market. The devices must not be supplied in Singapore.

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/export](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/export)

### Registered devices on consignment basis
Allows registered devices to be imported on a consignment basis before the dealer's licence is in place, under specific conditions.

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/consignment-registered](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/consignment-registered)

### Import for exhibition
Allows unregistered devices to be imported for the purpose of display or demonstration at exhibitions, without supply for patient use.

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/import-unregistered-devices-exhibition](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/import-unregistered-devices-exhibition)

### Unregistered pre-owned devices returned from maintenance/repair
A specific route for unregistered pre-owned devices being returned to Singapore after maintenance or repair overseas.

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/import-of-unregistered-pre-owned-devices-returned-from-maintenance-repair](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/import-of-unregistered-pre-owned-devices-returned-from-maintenance-repair)

### Pandemic SAR (PSAR-MD)
During public health emergencies, HSA may activate the Pandemic Special Access Route for emergency supply of medical devices that are not yet registered in Singapore.

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/PSAR-MD](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/registration/special-access-routes/PSAR-MD)
