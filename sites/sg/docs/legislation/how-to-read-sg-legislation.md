# How to Read Singapore Legislation

## Singapore Statutes Online (SSO)

All Singapore Acts, subsidiary legislation, and related instruments are freely available at:

**[sso.agc.gov.sg](https://sso.agc.gov.sg)**

SSO is maintained by the Attorney-General's Chambers. It publishes the consolidated, up-to-date text of all legislation. Unlike some jurisdictions, SSO integrates amendments into the main Act text, so the text you read reflects the current in-force version.

## Acts vs subsidiary legislation

| Type | What it is | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Act** | Primary legislation passed by Parliament | Health Products Act (Cap. 122D) |
| **Regulations / Subsidiary legislation** | Detailed rules made under the authority of an Act | HP(MD) Regulations 2010 |
| **Orders / Notifications** | Specific instruments (exemptions, appointed dates, etc.) | Various orders under the HPA |

For medical device regulation, the key documents are the **HPA** and the **HP(MD) Regulations**. HSA guidance documents are not legislation — they explain how HSA interprets and applies the legislation, but the legislation itself takes precedence.

## Reading the HP(MD) Regulations

When reading the HP(MD) Regulations, note:
- **"Act"** refers to the Health Products Act;
- **"Authority"** refers to HSA;
- **Schedules** (at the end of the regulations) contain the classification rules and application requirements — these are as legally binding as the main body of the regulations.
