# Open Consultations

HSA periodically conducts public consultations on proposed regulatory changes, new guidance documents, and policy initiatives affecting medical devices.

## Where to find open consultations

- **HSA website**: [hsa.gov.sg/announcements](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/announcements)
- **Ministry of Health consultations**: [moh.gov.sg](https://www.moh.gov.sg)
- **REACH (government feedback portal)**: [reach.gov.sg](https://www.reach.gov.sg)

## How to participate

Consultations typically involve:
1. HSA publishing a consultation paper or draft guidance document;
2. A defined public comment period (usually 4–8 weeks);
3. Submission of written feedback via the designated channel (email or online form);
4. HSA publishing a summary of feedback received and its response.

Industry associations (e.g. AMIS — Association of Medical and Biomedical Industries, Singapore) often collate and submit consolidated industry feedback.
