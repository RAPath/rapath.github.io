# Recent Regulatory Changes

This page tracks recent significant changes to Singapore's medical device regulatory requirements, including new or revised guidance documents, regulatory amendments, and changes to HSA's processes.

## How to stay current

The most reliable way to stay up to date with HSA regulatory changes is to:

1. **Monitor HSA announcements**: [hsa.gov.sg/announcements](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/announcements)
2. **Subscribe to HSA e-newsletters** (via the HSA website)
3. **Check the HSA guidance documents page** periodically for new or updated documents: [hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/guidance-documents](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/guidance-documents)
4. **Watch for IMDRF guidance publications** that HSA may adopt: [imdrf.org](https://www.imdrf.org)
