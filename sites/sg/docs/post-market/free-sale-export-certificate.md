# Free Sale Certificate and Export Certificate

## Free Sale Certificate (FSC)

A **Free Sale Certificate (FSC)** is an official document issued by HSA confirming that a specific medical device is registered in Singapore and is freely sold and marketed in the Singapore market. FSCs are typically required by overseas regulatory authorities as part of a market entry application in another country.

## Export Certificate

An **Export Certificate** is issued by HSA for medical devices that are exported from Singapore. It provides official confirmation of the device's registration status and the manufacturer's details.

## When are these certificates needed?

- When a Singapore registrant is applying to register the device in another ASEAN country or other export market;
- When a foreign regulatory authority requires documentary evidence of product approval in the country of origin or export;
- As part of an ASEAN CSDT submission.

## How to obtain

FSCs and Export Certificates are applied for through the HSA website and SHARE portal:

[hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/certificates](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/medical-devices/certificates)
