# DIGIPRiS — COFEPRIS Online Portal

**DIGIPRiS** (Sistema Integral de Gestión de Permisos, Registros e Insumos para la Salud) is the official online platform through which all interactions with COFEPRIS are conducted for medical devices.

## What DIGIPRiS is used for

All of the following are submitted through DIGIPRiS:

- New sanitary registration applications (standard and equivalency routes)
- Registration renewals
- Post-approval modifications
- Import permits and licences
- Aviso de funcionamiento (warehouse notice)
- Fee payment
- Status tracking of submitted applications

## Who can access DIGIPRiS

The **Mexico Registration Holder (MRH)** — the legal representative in Mexico — is responsible for accessing DIGIPRiS and managing submissions on behalf of the foreign manufacturer. Domestic manufacturers access the portal directly.

## Key features

- **Homoclave selection:** The portal guides applicants through selecting the correct application mode and code (*homoclave*) for their device and submission type. The homoclave system was streamlined by COFEPRIS in recent reforms to reduce overlapping codes.
- **Fee payment integration:** Government fees are paid through the portal at time of submission.
- **Application tracking:** Status updates are visible within DIGIPRiS once submitted.
- **Document uploads:** All dossier documents are uploaded in PDF format through the portal.

:::tip Pre-submission third-party review
For standard route submissions, COFEPRIS permits use of a designated pre-approving third-party agency to review the dossier before formal submission. This option is **not available** for the equivalency route. A successful pre-review can reduce the risk of deficiency letters and extend submission quality.
:::

## Practical tips

- Ensure all uploaded documents are in **Spanish** or are accompanied by **officially certified translations**.
- Check COFEPRIS's current list of accepted homoclave codes before submitting — codes are periodically updated.
- Keep proof of fee payment (pago de derechos) on file; this is required as part of the submission.
- DIGIPRiS may time out after extended periods of inactivity — save work frequently and consider breaking large dossier uploads into multiple sessions to avoid timeout errors.
