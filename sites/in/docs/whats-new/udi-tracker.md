# Udi Tracker

> **Official sources:** [CDSCO](https://cdsco.gov.in); [Official Gazette](https://egazette.gov.in); [SUGAM portal](https://sugam.cdsco.gov.in)

This page tracks Unique Device Identification (UDI) implementation timelines and requirements in India. UDI mandates are being phased in by CDSCO to align with international standards and enhance post-market surveillance, traceability, and vigilance reporting. Check this page for deadline announcements and device category-specific UDI requirements.
