# Schedule Expansion

> **Official sources:** [CDSCO](https://cdsco.gov.in); [Official Gazette](https://egazette.gov.in); [SUGAM portal](https://sugam.cdsco.gov.in)

This page tracks expansions to the Schedules of the Medical Devices Rules 2017 (MDR 2017), particularly Schedule 1 (in vitro diagnostic devices) and Schedule 2 (other medical devices). Schedule expansions reflect the addition of new device categories or changes to existing device classifications under CDSCO regulation.
