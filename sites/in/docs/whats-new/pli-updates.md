# Pli Updates

> **Official sources:** [CDSCO](https://cdsco.gov.in); [Official Gazette](https://egazette.gov.in); [SUGAM portal](https://sugam.cdsco.gov.in)

This page tracks updates to the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for medical devices under the Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers. The PLI Scheme aims to enhance manufacturing competitiveness and promote domestic production of medical devices. For the latest PLI announcements, refer to official CDSCO communications and the Ministry of Chemicals website.
