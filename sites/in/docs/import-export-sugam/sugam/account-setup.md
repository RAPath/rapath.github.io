# Creating & Managing Your SUGAM Account

## What is SUGAM?

The **SUGAM portal** ([sugam.cdsco.gov.in](https://sugam.cdsco.gov.in)) is CDSCO's online application and tracking platform for all central regulatory submissions, including import licences, manufacturing licences (Class C/D), clinical investigation permissions, and vigilance reports.

## Registration steps

1. Navigate to [sugam.cdsco.gov.in](https://sugam.cdsco.gov.in);
2. Select **"Register"** and choose entity type (IAR / Manufacturer / Importer);
3. Enter company details, contact information, and PAN/GSTIN;
4. Upload supporting documents (entity registration certificate);
5. Set login credentials and verify email;
6. CDSCO approves the registration (1–3 working days);
7. Login and complete your profile.

## Managing multiple products

A single SUGAM account can manage multiple import licences and applications for different products and manufacturers, provided the IAR is named on each application.

> **Official source:** [SUGAM portal help](https://sugam.cdsco.gov.in)
