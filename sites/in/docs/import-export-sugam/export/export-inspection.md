# Export Inspection

Indian-manufactured medical devices intended for export may require a **CDSCO export certificate** or **No Objection Certificate (NOC)**, and in some cases an **Export Inspection Council (EIC)** inspection certificate.

A **Certificate to Foreign Government (CFG)** is issued by CDSCO confirming the product is manufactured and/or sold in India in accordance with Indian regulations.

> **Official source:** [CDSCO — Export](https://cdsco.gov.in); Export Inspection Council of India
