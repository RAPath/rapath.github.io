# Who Must Appoint an IAR?

Every **foreign manufacturer** seeking a CDSCO import licence (Form MD-14 or MD-15) for notified medical devices must appoint an **Indian Authorised Representative (IAR)**.

The IAR must be named on the SUGAM application and on the issued licence. There is no exemption for foreign manufacturers with a direct India presence, unless they hold an Indian business registration (in which case they may act as their own IAR).

> **Official source:** Medical Devices Rules 2017, Rule 8; [CDSCO](https://cdsco.gov.in)
