To appoint an Indian Authorised Representative (IAR):

1. Select a suitable entity registered in India with relevant expertise in medical devices and regulatory compliance
2. Obtain a Power of Attorney (PoA) document signed by authorized personnel at the foreign manufacturer, granting the IAR authority to represent them before CDSCO
3. Ensure the IAR is not appointed by more than two foreign manufacturers for the same device category (to avoid conflict of interest)
4. Submit a formal application through SUGAM portal with the following documents:
   - Duly signed Power of Attorney
   - Proof of IAR's registration in India (Certificate of Incorporation or registration documents)
   - Details of IAR's experience and qualifications
   - Undertaking from IAR accepting the appointment
5. Submit with the device import license application or as an amendment if the IAR is already licensed

To change an IAR:

1. File a request through SUGAM portal detailing the current IAR and new IAR
2. Submit supporting authorization documents including new Power of Attorney from the foreign manufacturer
3. Provide justification for the change if applicable
4. The change becomes effective upon written approval from CDSCO

Timelines: IAR appointment approval is typically processed within 15-30 days of receipt of complete documentation. Changes to IAR can be processed as amendments to existing import licenses and are generally approved within similar timeframes.
