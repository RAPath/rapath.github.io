# Technical Documentation

Indian technical documentation requirements are based on the **IMDRF STED** (Summary Technical Documentation) format.

## Core technical file contents

- Device description and intended use;
- Summary of design and manufacturing;
- Essential Principles checklist (Schedule 4, MDR 2017);
- Risk management file (ISO 14971);
- Pre-clinical and clinical performance data;
- Labelling specimens;
- Post-market surveillance plan.

> **Official source:** Medical Devices Rules 2017, Schedule 4; IMDRF STED guidance

Essential Principles in Indian Context:

Schedule 4 of the Medical Devices Rules 2017 lists Essential Principles that all devices must comply with. These cover safety and performance, design and development, risk management, production and post-production, clinical evaluation, labelling and instructions, and post-market surveillance. A completed Essential Principles checklist must demonstrate how the device addresses each principle or provide justified exclusions.
