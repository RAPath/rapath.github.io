# Chapter Vii Labelling

This page summarises the key rules in this chapter of the Medical Devices Rules 2017 and links to the relevant primary source.

> **Official source:** [MDR 2017 — CDSCO](https://cdsco.gov.in/opencms/opencms/en/Medical-Device-Diagnostics/Medical-Device-Diagnostic-Rules/)

Chapter VII prescribes labeling requirements for medical devices sold in India, including mandatory use of both Hindi and English languages on labels, packaging, and instructional materials. English may be used as the primary language if Hindi translation is provided in a separate insert or document. Labels must include device name, manufacturer details, batch/serial numbers, expiry dates, storage instructions, warnings, contraindications, and directions for use. Requirements vary based on device classification and intended use.
