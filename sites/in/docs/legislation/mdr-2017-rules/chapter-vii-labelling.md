# Chapter Vii Labelling

This page summarises the key rules in this chapter of the Medical Devices Rules 2017 and links to the relevant primary source.

> **Official source:** [MDR 2017 — CDSCO](https://cdsco.gov.in/opencms/opencms/en/Medical-Device-Diagnostics/Medical-Device-Diagnostic-Rules/)

Chapter VII prescribes labeling requirements for medical devices, including mandatory use of Hindi and English languages on labels and packaging. Labels must include device name, manufacturer details, batch/serial numbers, expiry dates, storage instructions, warnings, contraindications, and directions for use. Requirements vary based on device classification and intended use.
