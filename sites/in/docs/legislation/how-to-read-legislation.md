# How To Read Legislation

Indian legislation is published in the **Official Gazette of India** ([egazette.gov.in](https://egazette.gov.in)). CDSCO circulars and office memoranda are published on the [CDSCO website](https://cdsco.gov.in).

The Medical Devices Rules 2017 is available at the [CDSCO medical devices rules page](https://cdsco.gov.in/opencms/opencms/en/Medical-Device-Diagnostics/Medical-Device-Diagnostic-Rules/).
