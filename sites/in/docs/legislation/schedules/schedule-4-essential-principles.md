# Schedule 4 Essential Principles

This page provides an overview of this Schedule of the Medical Devices Rules 2017 and links to the official text.

> **Official source:** [MDR 2017 — CDSCO](https://cdsco.gov.in/opencms/opencms/en/Medical-Device-Diagnostics/Medical-Device-Diagnostic-Rules/)

Schedule 4 of the Medical Devices Rules 2017 establishes the Essential Principles that medical devices must meet to ensure safety and performance. These principles cover: safety and performance requirements, design and manufacturing standards, risk management, clinical evidence requirements, labelling and traceability, post-market surveillance, and adverse event reporting. Devices must demonstrate compliance with applicable Essential Principles relevant to their classification and intended use.
