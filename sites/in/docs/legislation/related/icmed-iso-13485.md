# Icmed Iso 13485

> **Official source:** [Bureau of Indian Standards](https://www.bis.gov.in); [IMDRF](https://www.imdrf.org); [QCI ICMED](https://www.qcin.org/icmed)

ICMED (Indian Center for Medical Electronics and Devices) is a QCI-accredited body that provides ISO 13485:2016 certification for medical device manufacturers' quality management systems. ISO 13485 certification demonstrates compliance with international quality management standards and is recognized under MDR 2017 as evidence of GMP compliance. Manufacturers seeking to demonstrate quality management system competence can pursue ICMED ISO 13485 accreditation.
