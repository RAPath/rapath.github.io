# Imdrf Adoption

> **Official source:** [Bureau of Indian Standards](https://www.bis.gov.in); [IMDRF](https://www.imdrf.org); [QCI ICMED](https://www.qcin.org/icmed)

The International Medical Device Regulators Forum (IMDRF) develops consensus documents on medical device regulation, classification, and safety. While India is not an official IMDRF member state, CDSCO regulatory guidance and decision-making often reference IMDRF consensus documents on topics such as software as a medical device (SaMD), in vitro diagnostic devices (IVDs), clinical evaluation, and device classification. Manufacturers may reference IMDRF documents to support regulatory submissions and demonstrate alignment with international best practices.
