# Recent Amendments

Indian legislation, including amendments to MDR 2017, is published in the Official Gazette of India (egazette.gov.in). CDSCO circulars, office memoranda, and regulatory guidance are published on the CDSCO website (cdsco.gov.in).

The Medical Devices Rules 2017 is available at the [CDSCO medical devices rules page](https://cdsco.gov.in/opencms/opencms/en/Medical-Device-Diagnostics/Medical-Device-Diagnostic-Rules/).
