# Purchasing Controls

This section covers the **Schedule 5** requirements applicable to medical device manufacturers in India for this QMS element.

Schedule 5 Part I applies to all manufacturers (Class A–D). Schedule 5 Part II applies to Class C and D manufacturers only and includes additional requirements aligned with ISO 13485:2016.

> **Official source:** Medical Devices Rules 2017, Schedule 5
