# SFDA Announcements & Circulars

## Types of SFDA communications

The SFDA issues several types of official communications relevant to medical device manufacturers and ARs:

| Communication type | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Regulatory announcements** | Changes to registration requirements, new guidance, fee updates, portal changes |
| **Safety announcements** | Market-wide safety alerts, FSCAs initiated by SFDA, international safety signals |
| **Circulars** | Operational guidance, clarifications on existing requirements, procedural updates |
| **GHAD notifications** | System updates, new portal features, submission requirement changes |
| **Press releases** | Major policy announcements, MoU signings, IMDRF/GCC updates |

## Where to find SFDA announcements

- **SFDA website** — [www.sfda.gov.sa](https://www.sfda.gov.sa/) → News and Announcements
- **GHAD portal** — login notifications and system messages
- **SFDA social media** — official SFDA accounts on X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and other platforms (verify current accounts on SFDA website)
- **Your AR** — should monitor SFDA communications and relay relevant updates

## Language of announcements

SFDA announcements are primarily issued in **Arabic**. English summaries or translations may be provided for major regulatory changes, but not routinely. Manufacturers relying on their AR for SFDA monitoring should ensure the AR has Arabic-language capability for accurate interpretation.

## Setting up monitoring

Recommended monitoring practices:
1. Instruct your **AR** to monitor SFDA website and GHAD notifications weekly
2. Subscribe to SFDA email alerts if available on the website
3. Follow SFDA official social media accounts
4. Join relevant **industry association** mailing lists (e.g. Saudi Medical Devices Industry Association) that aggregate SFDA updates
