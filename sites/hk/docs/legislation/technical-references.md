# MDACS Technical References (TR Series)

> *HK-unique · TR series are the primary compliance reference*

Technical References (TRs) are normative guidance documents published by the MDD that define the standards and requirements for device conformity assessment, classification, and post-market monitoring. TRs serve as the primary regulatory reference for MDACS compliance and are functionally equivalent to regulations in a statutory framework.
