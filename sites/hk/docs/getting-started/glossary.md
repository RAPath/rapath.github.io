# Glossary

AMD
Applicable Medical Device — Class II, III, or IV general medical devices, or IVD Classes B, C, or D devices that are eligible for Department of Health procurement mandates; mandatory for Class II devices effective March 2026, with Class III/IV phased in thereafter
