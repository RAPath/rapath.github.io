# Local Responsible Person (LRP)

> *HK-unique framework · LRP is the key accountability holder*

## Who Can Be an LRP?

Any Hong Kong-registered company or sole proprietorship, including:
- The HK distributor appointed as LRP
- A dedicated regulatory affairs service provider
- A locally registered subsidiary of the manufacturer

Requirements: HK registered business address; technical capacity to fulfil LRP obligations.

## Pre-Market Obligations

| Obligation | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Listing application | Submit and manage MDACS listing via MDIS |
| Documentation | Compile and hold technical dossier |
| Labelling | Ensure HK labelling (bilingual where required) is compliant |
| MDD queries | Respond to queries during MDD review |

## Post-Market Obligations

| Obligation | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Adverse event reporting | Report within 10 or 30 days as applicable |
| FSCA coordination | Initiate and coordinate FSCAs |
| Complaint handling | Maintain documented complaint handling system |
| Device tracking | Track specified devices to user level |
| Change notification | Notify MDD of significant changes |
| MDD cooperation | Assist MDD-initiated investigations |
