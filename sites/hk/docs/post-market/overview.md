# Post-Market Overview

Once listed under MDACS, the **LRP** assumes ongoing post-market obligations:

| Obligation | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Adverse event repserious adverse events calendar days; other reportable events withinting | Report to MDD within 10 or 30 calendar days |
| FSCA management | Initiate and coordinate field safety corrective actions |
| Complaint handling | Maintain documented complaint handling system |
| Device traceability | Track specified devices to user level |
| Post-market surveillance | Systematically gather and analyse post-market safety data |
| Change notification | Notify MDD of significant changes to device or LRP |
| Service support | Arrange maintenance and service support where applicable |

The LRP is the **primary point of contact** for all post-market matters. MDD may conduct independent investigations and requires full LRP cooperation.

10 calendar days for serious adverse events; 30 calendar days for other reportable events.
