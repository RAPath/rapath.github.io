# Class I · II · III · IV — General Medical Devices

## Class I — Low Risk
Examples: Bandages, tongue depressors, examination gloves, stethoscopes, non-sterile gauze  
MDACS: ❌ Not eligible for listing. Class I devices fall outside the MDACS framework. They may be supplied in Hong Kong without MDACS registration provided they comply with general safety and quality standards under Hong Kong law.

## Class II — Moderate-Low Risk
Examples: Hypodermic needles, suction equipment, diagnostic imaging software, patient monitoring devices  
MDACS: ✅ Eligible. Subject to DH Stage C procurement mandate from March 2026.

## Class III — Moderate-High Risk
Examples: Lung ventilators, bone fixation plates, dialysis machines, implantable insulin pumps  
MDACS: ✅ Eligible. Higher documentation burden; ISO 13485 and clinical evaluation required.

## Class IV — High Risk
Examples: Heart valves, pacemakers, implantable defibrillators, cochlear implants, total joint replacements  
MDACS: ✅ Eligible. Most stringent requirements. Expedited reliance scheme is especially valuable given comprehensive reference-country dossiers typically available.
