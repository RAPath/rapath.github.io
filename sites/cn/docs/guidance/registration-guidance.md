Medical device registration in China follows a risk-based classification system (Class I, II, III). The registration process includes: application submission, technical review, clinical evaluation (when required), site inspection, and approval. Registration validity is 5 years from approval date for Class II and III devices, after which mandatory re-registration is required. Class I devices do not require re-registration. Expedited pathways exist for breakthrough devices and products addressing unmet clinical needs.
