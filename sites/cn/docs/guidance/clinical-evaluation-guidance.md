## Finding official guidance

NMPA technical guidance documents are published on:
- **CMDE website**: [www.cmde.org.cn](https://www.cmde.org.cn/) → 指导原则 (Technical Review Guidelines)
- **NMPA website**: [www.nmpa.gov.cn](https://www.nmpa.gov.cn/) → 医疗器械 → 法规文件

NMPA's clinical evaluation guidance requires different evidence strategies based on device risk class and type. For AI and machine learning-based devices, the 2022 AI Medical Device Registration Review Guidance Principle (人工智能医疗器械注册审查指导原则) provides specialized requirements including algorithm performance assessment and real-world validation. Key guidance documents address: clinical study design and endpoints, use of real-world evidence, assessment of algorithm performance, biocompatibility testing, and leveraging existing clinical data.
