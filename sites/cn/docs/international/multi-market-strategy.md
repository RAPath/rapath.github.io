*This page provides guidance on multi-market regulatory strategy when including China in your international launch plan. Detailed sequencing and data reuse strategies are being developed.*

## Why this comparison matters

Understanding how China's requirements differ from other major frameworks helps manufacturers:
- Identify data gaps early
- Sequence submissions efficiently
- Re-use clinical and technical data across markets
- Avoid surprises from China-unique requirements (e.g. China Agent, 5-year re-registration, clinical trial exemption list)
