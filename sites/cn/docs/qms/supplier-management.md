Supplier Management Requirements: Manufacturers must establish documented procedures to evaluate and approve all suppliers of materials, components, and services critical to device quality and safety. YY/T 0287-2017 requires: (1) Supplier Evaluation—assess supplier capability, quality systems, and compliance history before approval; (2) Purchasing Data—clearly specify purchased product requirements including drawings, specifications, process requirements, and quality standards; (3) Supplier Monitoring—conduct periodic audits (on-site or documentation review) to verify continued compliance; (4) Incoming Acceptance—establish acceptance criteria and inspection procedures for purchased products; (5) Supplier Records—maintain records of evaluations, approvals, audits, and corrective actions. NMPA expects evidence of supplier audits, certificates of analysis, supplier quality agreements, and documented responses to non-conformances. Suppliers must comply with applicable regulations in their jurisdiction and provide documentation supporting device traceability and batch control.
