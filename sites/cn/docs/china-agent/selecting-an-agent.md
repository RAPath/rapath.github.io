Add guidance on Agent selection criteria: (1) Must be a registered enterprise legal person (法人) in China with valid business license; (2) Should have prior NMPA agent experience or medical device industry knowledge; (3) Must have dedicated staff for post-market compliance and NMPA liaison; (4) Check Agent's track record via NMPA PDMS (publicly disclosed medical device system) for any enforcement actions or recalls; (5) Confirm Agent capacity to handle adverse event reporting and GMP inspection logistics; (6) Obtain written agreement specifying liability allocation, fee structure, and termination terms.
