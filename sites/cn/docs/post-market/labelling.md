## Key requirements

Labelling requirements are set out in MDSAR 2021 Articles 59-62 and the Technical Guidance for Medical Device Labelling. See [Legislation & Regulations](/cn/docs/legislation/mdsar-2021) for the full legislative index.

Medical device labelling in China must comply with MDSAR 2021 Articles 59-62 and the Technical Guidance for Medical Device Labelling. Key requirements include: primary language is Mandarin Chinese (simplified characters); English may appear alongside but not as primary; essential information includes: device name (registered name), intended use, contraindications, warnings and precautions, directions for use, storage conditions, shelf life, manufacturer/distributor information, registration certificate number, and unique device identification (UDI). For imported devices, Chinese translations must be accurate and complete. Class III devices require more detailed labelling. NMPA publishes labelling templates and examples on the medical device registration portal.
