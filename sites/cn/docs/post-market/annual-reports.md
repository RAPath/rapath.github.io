Annual reports in China must be submitted to NMPA within a specified timeframe following calendar year end (typically by March 31 of the following year). Reports must include: adverse event summaries, post-market surveillance data, corrective actions taken, manufacturing/quality changes, and updated product information. Class III devices typically require more detailed reporting than Class I devices. Manufacturers should reference the specific forms and templates available on the NMPA Medical Device Registration system (CDE portal) and the implementing Rules for Medical Device Registration (MDSAR 2021 Article 40-42).
