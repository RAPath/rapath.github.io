## How to stay current

The most reliable sources for NMPA regulatory changes are:

| Source | What it covers |
|---|---|
| [NMPA website — Medical Device section](https://www.nmpa.gov.cn/yaopin/ypjgdt/) | All NMPA orders, announcements, draft guidance |
| [CMDE website](https://www.cmde.org.cn/) | Technical review guidelines, new and draft guidance |
| [State Council (gov.cn)](https://www.gov.cn/) | State Council orders and regulations |

## Subscribing to NMPA updates

NMPA publishes updates via its official website and WeChat public account (微信公众号). Regulatory professionals monitoring the Chinese market should monitor these channels or work with a China Agent or regulatory consultant who tracks them.
