# Draft Guidance Open for Comment

NMPA and CMDE regularly publish draft technical guidance documents for public comment (公开征求意见). This page lists current open consultations.

---

## How NMPA guidance consultation works

1. CMDE publishes a draft guidance on its website with a defined comment (征求意见稿) public period, typically 30 days from publication date
2. Stakeholders (including overseas manufacturers and their China Agents) may submit written comments in Chinese via the CMDE website or designated email
3. CMDE reviews comments and may revise the draft before publishing the final guidance
4. Final guidance is published on the CMDE website and comes into force from the date of publication (or a specified future date)

---

## Finding current draft guidance

Check www.cmde.org.cn and navigate to the '征求意见' (Solicitation of Comments) or '通知公告' (Announcements) section for current open consultations. The CMDE website also sends notification emails to registered stakeholders when new draft guidance is published.
