# China Agent (代理人) — Introduction

:::info China-unique
The China Agent is a mandatory legal role with no direct parallel in the FDA, EU MDR, TGA, or HSA frameworks. It is one of the most important China-unique features for overseas manufacturers to understand.
:::

Under **Article 39 of MDSAR 2021**, every overseas manufacturer of medical devices sold in China must appoint a **China Agent (境内代理人)**. The Agent must be a legal entity registered in China (not an individual).

The China Agent is not merely an administrative contact — it bears **joint liability with the overseas manufacturer** for product quality and regulatory compliance.

---

## What does joint liability mean in practice?

Joint liability means that if the device causes harm, or if regulatory violations occur, NMPA can take enforcement action against **either** the overseas manufacturer or the China Agent (or both). The Agent cannot shield itself by claiming it was just a paperwork intermediary.

---

## Core functions of the China Agent

- **Registration holder**: The China Agent is named on the NMPA registration certificate as the domestic contact
- **Adverse event reporting**: Must submit adverse event reports to NMPA on behalf of the overseas manufacturer
- **Recall co-ordination**: Must co-ordinate and execute recalls within China
- **GMP co-operation**: Must facilitate NMPA overseas inspections
- **Label review**: Must ensure Chinese labelling complies with NMPA requirements
