## Regulatory basis

Requirements for software as a medical device (samd) are set out in:
- **MDSAR 2021** (State Council Order No. 739)
- Relevant NMPA technical guidelines published on [www.cmde.org.cn](https://www.cmde.org.cn/)
- Applicable YY/T and GB standards

SaMD-Specific Requirements: SaMD products are classified based on their intended use and risk level. NMPA recognizes three risk categories: low-risk, medium-risk, and high-risk SaMD. Each category carries different regulatory expectations. Documentation must include: (1) Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) evidence demonstrating controlled development processes; (2) Cybersecurity risk assessment and mitigation measures; (3) Software Validation and Verification (V&V) documentation covering functional requirements, integration testing, and system-level validation; (4) Post-market surveillance plans including adverse event monitoring and software update procedures. Refer to CMDE Technical Review Guidance for Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) for detailed dossier requirements by risk category.
