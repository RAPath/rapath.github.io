## After successful 품목신고

1. MFDS issues a **품목신고증 (Product Notification Certificate)**
2. Product may be marketed in Korea
3. Register the product in **KIMS** (UDI-K database)
4. Apply Korean-language labelling before commercial supply

## Ongoing obligations

Even after notification, the holder must:
- Maintain active business licence and GMP certificate
- Report adverse events
- Notify MFDS of changes via 변경신고
- Comply with all post-market obligations
