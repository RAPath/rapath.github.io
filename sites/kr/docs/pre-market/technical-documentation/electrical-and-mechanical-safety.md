Korea requires test reports from **MFDS-recognised testing laboratories** for applicable standards. Self-declaration is generally **not accepted**.

## Key standards

| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| IEC 60601-1 | General electrical safety for active medical devices |
| IEC 60601-1-2 | EMC |
| IEC 60601-1-6 | Usability engineering |
| IEC 60601-2-xx | Device-specific standards |
| ISO 11135 / 11137 | Sterilisation validation (EO / radiation) |
| ISO 11607 | Packaging for terminally sterilised devices |

## KC mark

Electrically-powered medical devices may also need a **KC mark** under the Electrical Appliances and Consumer Products Safety Control Act — separate from the MFDS 품목허가. See [KC mark](../../legislation/related-instruments/kc-mark-and-electrical-safety).
