## When the device is the lead

When the device is the lead

Submit 품목허가 (Marketing Authorization) under the Medical Devices Act to MFDS medical devices division
MFDS pharmaceutical division provides consultation on the drug component's safety and efficacy
Safety data must comprehensively address both device and drug interactions, including leachables testing, material compatibility, and any pharmacokinetic or pharmacodynamic interactions

## Common combination products

Drug-eluting stents · Antibiotic bone cement · Drug-coated balloons · Prefilled syringes with biologics · Transdermal drug-delivery patches

Interaction and compatibility testing

Applications must include:
• Leachables and extractables testing (ISO 10993-5 or equivalent) to assess chemical migration from device materials into the drug
• Material compatibility studies confirming the drug does not degrade or alter device performance
• Pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic interaction studies in relevant animal models or human studies as appropriate
• Stability data demonstrating shelf life under intended storage conditions for the combined product
• Risk assessment addressing potential device failure modes and impact on drug safety/efficacy
