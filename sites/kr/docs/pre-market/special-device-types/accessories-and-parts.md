---
title: "Accessories and Parts"
description: "Classification and regulation of accessories to medical devices in Korea."
sidebar_position: 10
---

Accessories are classified **independently** from the parent device based on the accessory's own risk profile and intended use with the parent device. An accessory may carry a different grade to its parent.

Each accessory requires its own **품목허가 or 품목신고** — it cannot be included in the parent device's authorisation unless MFDS explicitly accepts it.

**Spare parts** used to repair approved devices are generally not subject to pre-market requirements, but must be sourced from MFDS-recognised repair services.
