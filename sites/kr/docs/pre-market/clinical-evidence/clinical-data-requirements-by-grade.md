title: "Clinical Data Requirements by Grade"
description: "When and what clinical data MFDS requires for pre-market submissions."
sidebar_position: 1
---

| Grade | Clinical data requirement |
|---|---|
| **I** | Generally not required. Bench testing and literature sufficient. |
| **II** | Not always required. Literature may suffice for established device types. |
| **III** | Required. Clinical evaluation report or data from recognised clinical studies. |
| **IV** | Required. Robust clinical trial data or extensive published literature. |

For IVDs: Grade A/B require performance data; Grade C/D require formal performance evaluation studies.

## Acceptable forms of clinical data

1. Korean clinical trial data (highest weight)
2. Foreign clinical trial data (if population and use are relevant)
3. Published scientific literature (systematic review acceptable for established technologies)
4. Post-market clinical follow-up data

## When MFDS requires a Korean trial

- Foreign data not applicable to the Korean population
- First-in-class novel technology
- Specific MFDS safety/performance concerns

Note: Grade I devices may still require clinical data if they are novel or have new indications. The IVD grading system (A/B/C/D) differs from the general device grading system (I/II/III/IV) and follows separate performance evaluation pathways.
