Korea uses a **risk-based 4-grade classification system** for medical South (Grades I–IV) devices under the Medical Devices Act. MFDS publishes a comprehensive **Classification Table (분류 고시)** listing product types with their assigned grade.

## Steps to classify a device

1. Search the MFDS Classification Table by product type at [udiportal.mfds.go.kr](https://udiportal.mfds.go.kr)
2. Confirm the assigned Grade (I, II, III, or IV)
3. If unlisted, submit a **product classification inquiry (분류 민원)** to MFDS

## Risk factors considered

- Duration and degree of patient contact (transient / short-term / long-term)
- Degree of invasiveness (non-invasive → invasive → implantable)
- Whether the device delivers energy to the patient
- Potential consequences of device failure

## Relevant legislation
- Medical Devices Act, Articles 2–3
- MFDS Notification — Medical Device Classification and Grade Assignment
