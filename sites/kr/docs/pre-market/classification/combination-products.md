A **combination product** combines a medical device with a pharmaceutical, biological, or IVD reagent. Common examples: drug-eluting stents, prefilled syringes, drug-coated balloons.

## Lead regulator determination

MFDS determines the applicable regulatory pathway based on the **Primary Mode of Action (PMOA)**:

| PMOA | Regulatory pathway |
|---|---|
| Device component achieves primary effect | Medical Devices Act (MFDS device division) |
| Drug/biological component achieves primary effect | Pharmaceutical Affairs Act (MFDS pharma division) |

When the PMOA is unclear, submit a **combination product determination inquiry** to MFDS.

## Technical documentation requirements

Must address both device and drug aspects: device performance/safety data + drug pharmacological data (compatibility, stability, leachables), and a combined safety evaluation.
