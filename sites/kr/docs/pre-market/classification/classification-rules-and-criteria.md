## Primary method — product code lookup

The most straightforward approach: look up your product in the **MFDS Classification Table** which assigns a specific grade to each listed device type. Search via [udiportal.mfds.go.kr](https://udiportal.mfds.go.kr).

## When the table does not apply

Submit a **product classification inquiry (분류 민원)** via the MFDS eSubmission portal when:
- Your product is novel and not yet listed
- Your product could fall under multiple product types

## Key classification factors

| Factor | Determines |
|---|---|
| Intended use and body contact | Primary grade driver |
| Duration of contact | Transient / short-term / long-term |
| Invasiveness | Surface / body orifice / surgically invasive / implantable |
| Active vs non-active | Energy delivery raises grade |

## Software, accessories, and combination products

- **SaMD**: classified by intended function and risk — see [SaMD classification](./samd-classification)
- **Accessories**: classified independently from the parent device
- **Combination products**: MFDS determines which Act governs — see [Combination products](./combination-products)
