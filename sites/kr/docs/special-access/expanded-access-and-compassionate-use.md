**Expanded access** (also called compassionate use) allows patients with serious conditions and no alternative treatment options to access a medical device that has not yet received Korean 품목허가.

## Eligibility

Expanded access may be considered when:
- The patient has a **serious or life-threatening condition**
- No adequate approved treatment exists in Korea
- The patient cannot participate in a clinical trial
- Sufficient evidence exists of the device's safety and potential efficacy

## Process

1. The treating physician applies to MFDS for expanded access approval for a specific patient
2. MFDS reviews on a case-by-case basis
3. If approved, the device may be imported and used for that specific patient only

## Manufacturer's role

The foreign manufacturer (via Korean agent/importer) must cooperate with the MFDS application by providing safety and technical information.
