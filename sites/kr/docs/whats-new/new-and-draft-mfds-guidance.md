MFDS regularly publishes **new guidance documents (가이드라인)** and puts draft guidance out for public comment before finalisation.

## How to stay current

- Monitor the **MFDS website notifications (고시) section** for new publications
- Subscribe to MFDS email alerts (available to registered users of the MFDS portal)
- Check the **MFDS public comment portal** for draft guidance open for industry input

## Recent guidance publications (illustrative)

| Year | Document | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| 2024 | AI/ML Medical Device Guideline (revised) | Algorithm validation, PCCP, post-market monitoring |
| 2024 | Cybersecurity Guidance for Connected Devices | Cybersecurity requirements (initial release) |
