**Korean Industrial Standards (한국산업표준, KS)** are national standards developed by the **Korean Agency for Technology and Standards (KATS)**. Some KS standards are directly referenced in MFDS notifications as mandatory requirements for certain device types.

## KS standards in medical device regulation

Most KS standards for medical devices are **transpositions of IEC/ISO standards** with Korean-specific modifications. Examples:
- KS C IEC 60601-1 (equivalent to IEC 60601-1) — general safety for electrically-powered devices
- KS P ISO 10993 series — biocompatibility evaluation

## Finding applicable KS standards

The MFDS Classification Table specifies which KS or IEC/ISO standards apply to each device type. Test reports from **MFDS-recognised testing laboratories** must confirm conformance.
