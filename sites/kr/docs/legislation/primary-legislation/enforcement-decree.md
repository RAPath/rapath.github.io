The **Enforcement Decree (시행령)** is issued by the Korean Cabinet and provides detailed procedural provisions not contained in the primary Act.

## Key topics

- Specific application fee amounts for 품목허가 and GMP certification
- Composition and operation of MFDS advisory committees
- Criteria for MFDS designation of recognised testing laboratories
- Delegation of MFDS inspection authority
- Transitional provisions for new requirements
