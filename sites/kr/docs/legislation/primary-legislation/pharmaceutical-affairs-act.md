The **Pharmaceutical Affairs Act (약사법)** primarily governs pharmaceuticals in Korea but intersects with medical device regulation in several areas:

## Intersection areas

- **Combination products** — when the primary mode of action is pharmacological, the product is regulated as a drug under this Act, with MFDS medical device input
- **Pharmacies and retail medical devices** — distribution of certain lower-risk devices through pharmacies
- **Drug-device combination labelling** — labelling must satisfy both Acts where both apply
