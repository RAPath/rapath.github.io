The **MFDS GMP Standards Notification (의료기기 제조 및 품질관리 기준)** defines the detailed requirements of the **Korean Good Manufacturing Practice (KGMP)** system, largely aligned with **ISO 13485:2016**.

## Key chapters

- Management responsibility
- Resource management
- Product realisation (design, development, production)
- Measurement, analysis, and improvement (CAPA, internal audit)
- Korean-specific documentation requirements and GMP certificate application

## IVD-specific GMP

A separate chapter (or separate notification under the IVD Act) covers IVD-specific GMP requirements.
