## Timeframes

| Event type | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Death or serious deterioration — unexpected | **15 calendar days** from awareness |
| Death or serious deterioration — expected per risk management | **30 calendar days** |
| Malfunction with potential for death/serious injury if recurring | **30 calendar days** |

## When the clock starts

The clock starts from the date the **manufacturer or importer becomes aware** of the event — including from a complaint, a healthcare facility report, or any other source.

## Initial vs follow-up reports

- **Initial report**: submit within the timeframe with available information, even if investigation is incomplete
- **Follow-up report (수정 보고)**: submit with final investigation findings
