Medical device advertising in Korea is regulated by the Medical Devices Act and MFDS advertising standards notifications.

## General principles

- Claims must be **consistent with the approved intended use** (품목허가 / 품목신고 scope)
- Claims must not be **false, misleading, or exaggerated**
- Clinical claims must be supported by **evidence** in the approved technical documentation

## Grade III/IV — MFDS pre-approval of ads

For Grade III and IV devices, **general public advertising must be reviewed and approved by MFDS** before publication. Advertising to healthcare professionals has lighter requirements.

## Prohibited claims

- Efficacy claims beyond the approved intended use
- Superlatives ("best", "most effective") without substantiation
- Comparative claims without approved clinical data
- Claims linking the device to unapproved indications

## Relevant legislation
- Medical Devices Act, Article 24 · MFDS Notification — Advertising Standards
