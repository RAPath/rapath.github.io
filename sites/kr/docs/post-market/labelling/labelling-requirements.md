A key Korean requirement: **all medical device labelling must be in Korean (한국어)**. Devices without Korean labels may not be supplied in Korea.

## Mandatory label elements

- Product name (Korean; English optional)
- Manufacturer name and address
- Importer name and address (for imported devices)
- 품목허가 / 품목신고 number
- Lot / batch number or serial number
- Manufacturing date and expiry date (where applicable)
- Sterile indication (if applicable)
- Single-use indication (if applicable)
- **UDI-K barcode / data matrix** (for applicable devices)
- Intended use / indication summary
- Key warnings and contraindications

## Bilingual labelling

Bilingual (Korean + English) is permitted, but Korean must be present and equally prominent.

## Relevant legislation
- Medical Devices Act, Article 24 · MFDS Notification — Labelling Standards for Medical Devices
