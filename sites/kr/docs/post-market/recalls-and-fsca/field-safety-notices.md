A **Field Safety Notice (FSN)** is a communication to customers and healthcare professionals about a device safety issue, issued alongside or preceding an FSCA.

## Required FSN content (MFDS expectation)

- Product name, model, affected lot/batch numbers, UDI
- Description of the problem/hazard
- Risk assessment — potential consequences of the issue
- Recommended actions for healthcare facilities and/or patients
- Manufacturer/importer contact information
- Action required by the recipient (return, quarantine, modify, check)

## Language requirement

FSNs distributed in Korea must be in **Korean**.

## MFDS notification

A copy of the FSN must be submitted to **MFDS** at the time of distribution.
