Holders of Grade III and IV 품목허가 must submit **periodic safety reports** to MFDS summarising post-market safety experience.

## Reporting frequency

Specified by MFDS — typically **annually** for the first few years post-approval, then every 2–3 years for established devices.

## Report content

- Summary of all adverse events reported during the period
- Analysis of PMS data (literature, registry data, complaints)
- International regulatory actions (foreign recalls, safety alerts)
- Assessment of the benefit-risk balance
- Proposed actions if a safety signal is identified
