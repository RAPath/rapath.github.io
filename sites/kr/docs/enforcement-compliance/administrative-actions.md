## MFDS administrative action toolkit

| Action | Description | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Warning letter | Written warning requiring corrective action | Minor violation |
| Business improvement order | MFDS orders specific corrective actions | Moderate violation |
| Product sale suspension | MFDS orders suspension of sales for specific products | Safety risk or significant GMP failure |
| Licence suspension | Temporary suspension of manufacturer or importer licence | Serious violation |
| Licence cancellation | Permanent cancellation of licence | Very serious violation or fraud |
| 품목허가 cancellation | Cancellation of product authorization | Device unsafe or application fraud |
| Import ban | Prohibition of importation for specific products or from specific manufacturers | Serious safety concern |
| Mandatory recall | MFDS orders a product recall | Imminent patient risk |

## Appeals

Manufacturers and importers may **appeal** MFDS administrative actions through:
1. Internal MFDS review (이의신청)
2. Administrative appeals tribunal (행정심판)
3. Administrative courts (행정소송)
