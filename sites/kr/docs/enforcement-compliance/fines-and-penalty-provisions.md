## Administrative fines (과태료)

Administrative fines (not criminal) for less serious violations of the Medical Devices Act. Common triggers:

- Failure to report an adverse event within the required timeframe
- Failure to maintain required records
- Failure to register in KIMS / UDI-K by the required date
- Failure to notify MFDS of a change (변경신고)

Fine amounts vary by violation type and are specified in the Enforcement Decree.

## Criminal penalties (벌칙)

More serious violations may result in **criminal prosecution** under the Medical Devices Act:

| Offence | Maximum penalty |
|---|---|
| Manufacturing / importing without a licence | Imprisonment up to 5 years or fine up to ₩50 million |
| Selling without 품목허가 / 품목신고 | Imprisonment up to 3 years or fine up to ₩30 million |
| False or fraudulent application | Imprisonment up to 5 years or fine up to ₩50 million |
| Obstructing a lawful MFDS inspection | Fine |

## Corporate liability

Both the **individual employee** responsible for the violation and the **company** may face penalties — the company can be fined even if only an individual is prosecuted.

Fine amounts for common violations: Failure to report adverse events within 15 days may incur fines up to ₩5 million. Failure to maintain required records or register in KIMS/UDI-K may result in fines up to ₩3 million. Failure to notify MFDS of product changes may incur fines up to ₩2 million. Refer to the Medical Devices Act Enforcement Decree Article 85 for the complete schedule of administrative fines.
