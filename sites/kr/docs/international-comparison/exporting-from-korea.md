Korean manufacturers exporting medical devices may need an **MFDS export certificate (수출 의료기기 증명서)** as proof of regulatory status for importing countries.

## Types of export certificates

| Certificate | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Certificate of Free Sale (수출 의료기기 증명서) | Confirms device is freely sold in Korea and not banned |
| GMP Certificate for export | Confirms the manufacturer holds a valid KGMP certificate |
| Certificate of Compliance | Confirms device meets Korean regulatory requirements |

## Who needs an export certificate?

Most importing countries (EU, US, ASEAN, etc.) require an export certificate or Certificate of Free Sale as part of their import registration / market approval process for foreign devices.

## How to obtain

Apply via the **MFDS eSubmission portal (의료기기 전자민원)**:
- The manufacturing site must be KGMP-certified
- The product must have a valid 품목허가 or 품목신고 (or be an export-only device meeting requirements)
- The product must not be banned in Korea

MFDS typically issues export certificates within a few business days.
