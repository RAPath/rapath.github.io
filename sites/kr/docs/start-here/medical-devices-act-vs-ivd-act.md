Korea has **two separate primary Acts** governing medical products. Choosing correctly is the essential first step.

## Decision logic

```
Is the product used outside the body to examine human specimens?
├─ YES → In Vitro Diagnostics Medical Devices Act (IVD Act)
└─ NO  → Does it diagnose, treat, prevent, or alleviate disease/disability?
          ├─ YES → Medical Devices Act
          └─ NO  → Not a regulated medical device
```

## Key differences

| Feature | Medical Devices Act | IVD Act |
|---|---|---|
| Classification grades | I, II, III, IV | A, B, C, D |
| Clinical evidence | Clinical evaluation or clinical trial data | Performance evaluation or analytical/clinical study data |
| Effective date | Long established | May 2021 |

## Combination products

Contact MFDS for a product determination when a device crosses both Acts.
