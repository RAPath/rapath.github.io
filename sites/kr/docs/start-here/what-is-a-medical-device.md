title: "What is a Medical Device?"
description: "The legal definition of a medical device under Korea's Medical Devices Act."

---

Under Korea's **Medical Devices Act (의료기기법)**, a medical device is any instrument, machine, apparatus, material, software, or other article intended by the manufacturer for use in humans for diagnosing, curing, treating, alleviating, or preventing disease or disability, or for investigating, replacing, or modifying anatomical structure or physiological function.

## Key points

- **Intended use** determines classification, not the product alone
- **Software** can be a medical device (SaMD) if it meets the intended use criteria
- **Accessories** to medical devices are regulated as medical devices in their own right
- IVD devices are excluded and regulated under the separate **IVD Act**

## Relevant legislation
- Medical Devices Act, Article 2 (Definitions)
- MFDS Notification — Medical Device Classification and Grade Assignment
