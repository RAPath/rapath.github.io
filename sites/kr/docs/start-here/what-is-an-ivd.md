An **in vitro diagnostic (IVD) medical device** is any medical device — including reagents, calibrators, instruments, and software — intended for **in vitro examination of human-derived specimens** to provide information about physiological or pathological states, congenital abnormalities, treatment compatibility, or therapeutic monitoring.

## Dedicated IVD Act

Korea introduced the **In Vitro Diagnostics Medical Devices Act (체외진단의료기기법)** effective **May 2021**, separating IVD regulation from the broader Medical Devices Act. IVD devices have their own 4-grade classification system (Grades A, B, C, D).

## Relevant legislation
- In Vitro Diagnostics Medical Devices Act, Article 2
