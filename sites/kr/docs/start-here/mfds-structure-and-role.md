The **Ministry of Food and Drug Safety (MFDS, 식품의약품안전처)** is Korea's central authority for medical device regulation, elevated to Ministry level in 2013.

## Core responsibilities

- Market authorization (품목허가) and notification (품목신고) processing
- KGMP certification for domestic manufacturers; recognition of foreign GMP certificates
- Post-market surveillance, adverse event monitoring, recalls
- UDI-K administration and KIMS database
- Legislation (고시) and guidance publication
- Enforcement and inspections
