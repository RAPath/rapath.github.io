| Year | Change | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| 2019 | IVD Act enacted | Separate regulation for IVDs established; effective May 2021 |
| 2021 | IVD Act fully effective | All IVD manufacturers transition from Medical Devices Act |
| 2022 | UDI-K Phase 1 | Grade IV devices require UDI-K labelling and KIMS registration |
| 2023 | Innovative Medical Device scheme expanded | Faster pathway for innovative devices |
| 2024 | AI/ML guidance updated | Post-market performance monitoring requirements for AI algorithms |
| 2024 | eIFU provisions introduced | Electronic IFU permitted for professional-use devices |
| 2024 | UDI-K Phase 2 | Grade III devices require UDI-K |

:::info Stay current
See [What's New](../whats-new/recent-regulatory-changes) for the latest updates.
:::
