The following are **not** regulated as medical devices under the Medical Devices Act:

| Category | Governing law |
|---|---|
| Pharmaceuticals | Pharmaceutical Affairs Act |
| IVD devices | In Vitro Diagnostics Medical Devices Act |
| Cosmetics | Cosmetics Act |
| General health products | Health Functional Foods Act |
| General PPE | Industrial Safety & Health Act |
| Veterinary devices | Veterinary-related legislation |

## Borderline products

When a product's status is unclear, submit a **product classification inquiry (분류 민원)** to MFDS before investing in approval.

Common borderline areas: wellness apps/wearables, cosmetic-device combinations, drug-device combinations.
