## Manufacturers (제조업자)
Must hold: manufacturer licence (제조업 허가), KGMP certification, 품목허가/신고 per product, UDI-K/KIMS registration.

## Foreign Manufacturers
Do **not** need a Korean manufacturer licence but must:
- Appoint a **Korean agent (한국 대리인)** — typically the Korean importer
- Obtain a **Foreign GMP Certificate (해외제조소 인증)** recognised by MFDS

## Importers (수입업자)
Must hold: importer licence (수입업 허가), 품목허가/신고 for each imported product, ensure valid foreign GMP certificate, apply Korean-language labels.

## Distributors & Retail Dealers
Must **register** with MFDS before distributing. Must store devices properly and report adverse events.

## Repair Business Operators
Must register as a **repair business (수리업)**.
