Purchasing and supplier controls ensure that **externally provided products and services** that affect device quality are controlled (ISO 13485:2016 Section 7.4).

## Key requirements

- **Approved supplier list (ASL)** — maintained list of suppliers evaluated and approved
- **Supplier evaluation criteria** — defined criteria based on the supplier's ability to meet requirements
- **Purchasing information** — purchasing documents specify requirements clearly
- **Verification of purchased product** — incoming inspection or other verification activities
- **Supplier monitoring** — ongoing monitoring of supplier performance (re-evaluation at defined intervals)
- **Critical suppliers** — higher controls for suppliers of critical components or services

## Special focus: sterile device manufacturers

For sterile devices, sterilisation service providers are treated as **critical suppliers** requiring more rigorous evaluation and monitoring.
