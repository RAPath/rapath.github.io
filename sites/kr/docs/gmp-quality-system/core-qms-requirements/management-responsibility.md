Management responsibility is a foundational chapter of both **KGMP** and **ISO 13485:2016** (Section 5).

## Key requirements

- **Quality policy** — documented, communicated, and understood at all levels
- **Quality objectives** — measurable and aligned with the quality policy
- **Management representative** — designated with authority for QMS maintenance
- **Management review** — scheduled at planned intervals; inputs and outputs documented
- **Resource provision** — top management ensures adequate resources for QMS maintenance

## During MFDS inspection

MFDS inspectors will review:
- Quality manual and quality policy
- Management review records (minutes, action items, closure evidence)
- Organisational chart and delegation of quality authority
- Evidence that quality objectives are monitored and achieved
