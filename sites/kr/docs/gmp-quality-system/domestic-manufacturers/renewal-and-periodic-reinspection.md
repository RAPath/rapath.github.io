## KGMP certificate validity

KGMP certificates have a **validity period of approximately 3 years**. Renewal application must be submitted before expiry.

## Renewal process

1. Submit renewal application via MFDS eSubmission portal before expiry
2. MFDS schedules a **periodic re-inspection**
3. Re-inspection may be lighter than initial certification if quality record is good

## Periodic surveillance inspections

MFDS may conduct **unannounced or scheduled surveillance inspections** at any time during the certificate validity period.

## Lapsed certification consequences

- Cannot supply new products to the Korean market
- Existing supply chain stock: short transition period may apply
- MFDS may suspend or cancel associated 품목허가
