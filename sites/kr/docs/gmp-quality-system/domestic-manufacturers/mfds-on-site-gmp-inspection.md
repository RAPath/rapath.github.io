## Inspection coverage

MFDS on-site inspections cover:

- **Document review** — quality manual, procedures, records
- **Facility tour** — production areas, cleanrooms, storage, environmental monitoring
- **Process observation** — manufacturing processes, in-process controls
- **Equipment verification** — calibration records, maintenance logs
- **Personnel interviews** — key QMS personnel (QA Manager, production manager)
- **Record review** — batch records, CAPA records, complaint records, internal audit reports

## Duration

Typically **1–3 days** depending on facility size and product complexity.

## Nonconformities

If nonconformities are identified:
- Manufacturer provides a **CAPA plan** within a specified timeframe
- Implements corrective actions
- Submits evidence of closure to MFDS
- Minor nonconformities: resolved without re-inspection
- Major nonconformities: may require a follow-up inspection
