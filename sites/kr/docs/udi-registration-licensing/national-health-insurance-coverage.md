Korea has a **National Health Insurance (NHI) system** administered by the **National Health Insurance Service (NHIS)**. Device reimbursement (급여 등재) is managed by **HIRA (Health Insurance Review and Assessment Service)** and is separate from MFDS 품목허가.

## The two-step process

| Step | Authority | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| MFDS 품목허가 | MFDS | Legal authority to sell the device in Korea |
| NHI listing | HIRA / NHIS | Authorization for NHI reimbursement |

For most devices, NHI listing is **commercially critical** — most Korean hospital purchases rely on NHI reimbursement.

## How to apply for NHI listing

1. Obtain MFDS 품목허가
2. Submit NHI coverage application to **HIRA**
3. HIRA evaluates **clinical effectiveness and cost-effectiveness**
4. NHIS determines the **reimbursement price**
5. If approved, device is listed in the NHI benefit schedule
