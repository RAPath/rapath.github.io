## Compliance timeline

| Grade | UDI-K labelling required from | KIMS registration required from |
|---|---|---|
| **Grade IV** | January 2022 | January 2022 |
| **Grade III** | January 2024 | January 2024 |
| **Grade II** | TBD (expected 2025–2026) | TBD |
| **Grade I** | TBD | TBD |

:::note
Confirm current dates with MFDS — the schedule may be updated.
:::

## What "compliance" means by the applicable date

1. DI obtained from an issuing agency (GS1 Korea or HIBCC)
2. Device registered in KIMS with all required data
3. UDI-K labels applied to all units distributed in Korea from that date

## Existing stock at compliance date

MFDS may allow a transition period for existing stock. Check the relevant MFDS notification for specific transition provisions.
