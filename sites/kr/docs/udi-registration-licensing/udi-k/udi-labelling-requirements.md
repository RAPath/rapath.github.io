## Machine-readable format (barcode/data matrix)

The complete UDI-K (DI + applicable PI) must appear on the device label in a **machine-readable format**:

- **GS1 DataMatrix** (most common — compact, high data density)
- **GS1-128 barcode** (linear)
- **HIBC formats** (for HIBCC issuing agency users)

## Human-readable form

For most device types, the UDI-K must also appear in **human-readable form** adjacent to the barcode/data matrix.

## Packaging levels

Different UDI-K labels are required for:
- **Unit of use** — individual device
- **Intermediate packaging** (e.g., box of 10)
- **Case / shipping container**

## Implantable devices

UDI-K must appear on device packaging AND the production identifier (serial + lot + expiry) must be **recorded in the patient's hospital record**.
