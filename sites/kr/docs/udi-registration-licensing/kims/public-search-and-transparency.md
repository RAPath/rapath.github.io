## Public access

The KIMS database is publicly accessible at [udiportal.mfds.go.kr](https://udiportal.mfds.go.kr). Anyone can search for:

- Device name and manufacturer
- 품목허가 / 품목신고 number
- Device grade
- UDI / DI information
- Recall history
- Adverse event summary (where publicly disclosed)

## Why transparency matters

- **Healthcare facilities** — verify a device has a valid Korean authorisation before purchasing
- **Patients** — look up information about a device used in their care
- **Distributors** — confirm product compliance status
- **MFDS** — conduct targeted market surveillance using product traceability data
