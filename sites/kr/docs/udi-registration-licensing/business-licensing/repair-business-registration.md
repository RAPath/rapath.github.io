Entities that **repair medical devices** in Korea must register as a **repair business (수리업)** with MFDS.

## Requirements

- Business registration in Korea
- Qualified personnel capable of performing the repairs
- Adequate tools, equipment, and facilities for the repair scope

## Scope

Required for repair activities on Grade II, III, and IV devices. Grade I may have lighter requirements.

## Obligations

- Only repair devices within their qualified scope
- Use genuine parts or approved alternatives
- Keep repair records
- Report adverse events discovered during repair
