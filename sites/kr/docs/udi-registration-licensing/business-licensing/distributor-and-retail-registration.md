Entities distributing medical devices in Korea must register with MFDS before commencing distribution. Registration is mandatory even if the distributor is also the product's approval holder (품목허가/신고 holder).

## Who must register?

- **Distributors (판매업자)** — selling devices to healthcare facilities or other distributors
- **Rental dealers (임대업자)** — renting medical devices to users

## Registration requirements

- Business registration in Korea
- Designated compliance responsible person
- Adequate storage facilities (temperature control etc. as applicable)

## Ongoing obligations

- Only supply devices with valid 품목허가 / 품목신고
- Maintain storage conditions per manufacturer requirements
- Keep distribution records for traceability
- Report adverse events they become aware of
