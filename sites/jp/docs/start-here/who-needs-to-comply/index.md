# Who Needs to Comply?

Multiple parties in the supply chain bear regulatory obligations under the PMD Act. Unlike the EU MDR, where the manufacturer bears primary responsibility, Japan's system centres the obligations on the **Marketing Authorization Holder (MAH)** — who must be Japan-domiciled.

| Role | Primary obligation |
| --- | --- |
| [Marketing Authorization Holder (MAH)](./marketing-authorization-holders) | Holds the approval/certification; bears primary post-market obligations |
| [Domestic Manufacturers](./domestic-manufacturers) | Must hold a manufacturing licence; maintain QMS per MO 169 |
| [Foreign Manufacturers](./foreign-manufacturers) | Must register with MHLW via FMR; maintain QMS subject to PMDA inspection |
| [Registered Certification Bodies (RCBs)](./registered-certification-bodies) | Conduct Ninsho (Class II) certifications under MHLW oversight |
| [Distributors and Retailers](./distributors-retailers) | Distribution licence; record-keeping; reporting obligations |
