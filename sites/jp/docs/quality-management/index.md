# Quality Management (QMS)

Japan's quality management system requirements for medical device manufacturers are set out in **Ministerial Ordinance 169** (QMS Ordinance). Compliance is mandatory for all manufacturing sites supplying devices to the Japanese market — domestic and foreign.

This section covers the QMS Ordinance requirements, manufacturing and facility registration, and PMDA inspection processes.
