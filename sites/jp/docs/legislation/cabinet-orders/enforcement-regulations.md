# Enforcement Regulations (PMD Act)

## What the Enforcement Regulations cover

The PMD Act Enforcement Regulations (薬機法施行規則 / Yakki-hō Shikōkisoku) are a Ministerial Ordinance-level instrument that provides the procedural details for implementing the PMD Act. They specify:

- Application forms and required documents for manufacturing licences, MAH licences, Shonin applications, and Ninsho applications
- Administrative procedures for licence renewals, change notifications, and withdrawals
- Timing and format requirements for adverse event reports
- Forms for MHLW notifications relating to recalls

## Key provisions relevant to medical devices

| Subject | Enforcement Regulation reference |
| --- | --- |
| Manufacturing licence application requirements | Articles on manufacturing business applications |
| MAH licence application requirements | Articles on manufacturing sales business applications |
| Shonin application content | Articles referencing required dossier content |
| Adverse event report format | Articles on safety reporting forms |
| Recall notification format | Articles on recall/corrective action reporting |

The Enforcement Regulations are updated more frequently than the PMD Act itself, as they deal with procedural details that need to track changing administrative practices.
