# DAPI — Swissmedic Product Database

## Overview
**DAPI** (Database of Authorised Products and Information) is Swissmedic's database of authorised therapeutic products including medical devices. DAPI is the Swiss equivalent of EUDAMED — smaller in scope but serving the same function: Swiss market registration and notification.

## Public vs Restricted Data
**Publicly accessible**: device name, model, manufacturer name and country, CH REP details, device class, authorisation status and validity dates.
**Restricted (registrant/Swissmedic only)**: DoC details, NB certificate numbers, complete technical specifications, vigilance report references.

## How to Register in DAPI
1. Create a user account with Swissmedic
2. Log in to the DAPI submission portal at **[www.swissmedic.ch (DAPI is managed directly by Swissmedic)](https://www.dapi.ch)**
3. Submit device information using the appropriate form (notification or full registration)
4. Attach required documents (DoC, NB certificates, labelling)
5. Pay the applicable registration fee

## DAPI and UDI
UDI data must be registered in DAPI as part of the Swiss UDI system, mirroring EU MDR UDI requirements. See [UDI in Switzerland](../../post-market/labelling/udi-in-switzerland).
