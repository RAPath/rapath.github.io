# Transitional Timeline Tracker

:::caution Verify dates directly with Swissmedic
Transitional dates have been amended before. Always confirm the current operative dates directly with Swissmedic before making compliance decisions.
:::

## MedDO Transitional Events

| Event | Reference Date |
|---|---|
| MedDO entered into force | 26 May 2021 |
| Last date to place on Swiss market under MDD/AIMDD certificates (general devices) | 26 May 2024 |
| End of sell-off period for devices already on the market (general) | 26 May 2023–5 years, depending on the certificate classification and NB assessment; most are issued for 3 with surveillance audits |
| Implantable Class III and Class IIb — earlier phased dates | Check MedDO Art. 103 |

## IVDO Transitional Events

## NB Certificate Validity
EU MDR/IVDR NB certificates are typically issued for **5 years**. Plan certificate renewal at least **12 months before expiry** to allow NB processing time.

| Action | Recommended Timing |
|---|---|
| Contact NB about renewal | 12–18 months before expiry |
| Submit renewal application | 12 months before expiry |
| Receive renewed certificate | Before expiry |
| Update DAPI with new certificate | On receipt |
