# Glossary of Key Terms

DAPI
Swissmedic's product database of authorised therapeutic products and devices. Manufacturers and CH REPs register or notify medical devices through DAPI at dapi.ch before market placement in Switzerland.

'DAPI Notification

Add the following entries to the glossary:

'BASEC
Swiss Federal Commission for Research involving Human Beings. The ethics committee responsible for approving clinical investigations (ClinO-MD) in Switzerland.'

'GSPR
General Safety and Performance Requirements. The essential safety and performance standards that medical devices must meet under MedDO/IVDO, covering design, risk management, sterility, biocompatibility, labelling, and post-market surveillance.'

'MRA
Mutual Recognition Agreement. An agreement between Switzerland and the EU recognising the equivalence of conformity assessment and regulatory oversight, permitting CE-marked devices to be placed on the Swiss market under certain conditions without separate DAPI registration.'

'TPA
Therapeutic Products Act (Bundesgesetz über Arzneimittel, SR 812.21). The primary Swiss federal law governing medical devices and therapeutic products.'
