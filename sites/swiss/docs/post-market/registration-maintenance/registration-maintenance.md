# Registration Maintenance

## Overview
Maintaining an accurate and current DAPI registration is an ongoing post-market obligation. Manufacturers and CH REPs must promptly notify Swissmedic of significant changes and ensure all registration data remains accurate.

## Ongoing Obligations
- **Significant changes**: Notify Swissmedic via DAPI when changes occur that affect intended purpose, safety/performance, or validity of conformity assessment — see [Certificate Maintenance](../../pre-market/swissmedic-registration/certificate-maintenance)
- **CH REP changes**: Update DAPI promptly when CH REP mandate terminates or a new CH REP is appointed
- **NB certificate renewals**: Update DAPI when NB certificates are renewed
- **Address changes**: Update manufacturer and CH REP addresses promptly
- **Device withdrawal**: Update DAPI if a device is withdrawn from the Swiss market; retain technical documentation for the full retention period (10 or 15 years)

## Annual Maintenance Fees
Annual maintenance fees may apply depending on device class — see [Registration Fees and Timelines](../../pre-market/swissmedic-registration/fees-and-timelines) and the current Swissmedic fee schedule.
