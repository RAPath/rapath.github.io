# GRLS — State Register Guide

## What is the GRLS?

The **GRLS** (Государственный реестр медицинских изделий — State Register of Medical Devices) is Russia's official database of all registered medical devices. It is maintained by the Ministry of Health and accessible at [grls.rosminzdrav.ru/GRLS.aspx](https://grls.rosminzdrav.ru/GRLS.aspx).

Every medical device that has been approved for placement on the Russian market has an entry in the GRLS. The Registration Certificate number issued by Roszdravnadzor corresponds to the GRLS entry.

## What a GRLS entry contains

Each GRLS record typically includes:
- **Registration Certificate number** (RZN number)
- **Device name** — trade name and/or common name in Russian
- **Manufacturer name and address**
- **Russian Authorized Representative** name and address
- **Device class** (I, IIa, IIb, III)
- **OKPD-2 classification code**
- **Intended purpose** — as approved
- **Registration date and validity**
- **Scope of registration** — models and configurations covered

## Searching the GRLS

The GRLS search interface (in Russian) allows you to search by:
- Registration Certificate number (RZN number)
- Device name
- Manufacturer name
- OKPD-2 code

Note: The GRLS interface is in Russian. Browser-based translation tools can assist in navigating the site.

## GRLS vs EAEU database

The GRLS covers devices registered under the **national** pathway. Devices registered under the **EAEU** pathway are listed in the separate EAEU database maintained by the Eurasian Economic Commission at [eec.eaeunion.org](https://eec.eaeunion.org/). From 2027, new registrations will appear in the EAEU database rather than the GRLS.

## Keeping your GRLS entry up to date

Manufacturers must ensure their GRLS entry remains accurate. Changes to the device, manufacturer, or authorized representative must be notified to Roszdravnadzor and reflected in the GRLS. An inaccurate or outdated GRLS entry can create compliance issues.
