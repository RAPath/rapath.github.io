# Recent Regulatory Changes

> *CDRRHR · FDA Philippines · 2022–2026 developments*

## 2022

### FDA Circular 2022-008 — Abridged ASEAN NRA Pathway

Published in 2022, **FDA Circular 2022-008** introduced the abridged CMDR pathway for devices already approved by a recognised ASEAN NRA:
- **30 working-day** processing from payment receipt
- Leverages ASEAN NRA technical assessment
- Identical device specification requirement
- CDRRHR retains right to withhold approval if safety concerns exist

This circular significantly reduces the registration burden for devices with existing ASEAN approvals.

## Ongoing Developments

### ASEAN AMDD Harmonisation

The Philippines continues to participate in ASEAN medical device harmonisation activities:
- ASEAN CSDT updates are incorporated as they are agreed at the ASEAN level
- Philippines participates in ASEAN regulatory convergence working groups
- Further expansion of reliance frameworks is under ongoing discussion

### CDRRHR Enforcement Activity

CDRRHR and the Bureau of Customs have increased coordination on interception of unregistered medical devices at ports of entry. Compliance with CMDN/CMDR registration requirements before shipment is essential.
