# CMDR — Class B/C/D Registration

> *PH-Unique · Certificate of Medical Device Registration · Class B · C · D*

## What Is the CMDR?

The **Certificate of Medical Device Registration (CMDR)** is the full registration approval for **Class B, C, and D medical devices** in the Philippines. It requires a comprehensive technical review and full ASEAN CSDT dossier submission.

## CMDR Application Requirements

### 1. License to Operate (LTO)
Valid LTO for the applicant entity (importer, manufacturer, or distributor) is a prerequisite.

### 2. ASEAN CSDT — Full Format

The CMDR application must use the **ASEAN Common Submission Dossier Template (CSDT)** full format. This ensures compatibility with other ASEAN market submissions.

| CSDT Section | Content |
|-------------|---------|
| Administrative | Applicant, manufacturer, device identification, application type |
| Device description | Intended purpose, operating principle, materials, configurations |
| Classification justification | Class assignment and applicable ASEAN AMDD rules |
| Essential principles checklist | Mapping each requirement to compliance evidence |
| Design verification and validation | Bench tests, performance testing |
| Risk management | ISO 14971 summary |
| Clinical / performance evaluation | CER (general devices) or performance evaluation (IVDs when applicable) |
| Labelling | Philippines-compliant labelling per AO 2018-0002 |
| QMS evidence | ISO 13485 certificate |
| Manufacturing information | Site details, GMP evidence |

### 3. Letter of Authorization — PH-Unique Requirements

The **Letter of Authorization (LOA)** is a critical document with specific Philippines requirements:
- Must be from the **legal manufacturer**
- Must include a **notarised declaration** from the manufacturer confirming:
  - The device meets the essential principles of safety and performance
  - The technical data in the dossier is accurate and complete
  - The manufacturer authorises the named Philippines entity to hold the registration
- **Apostille or authentication** may be required for the notarisation depending on the country of origin — confirm with CDRRHR

### 4. Free Sale Certificate (FSC)
From the regulatory authority of the country of manufacture. Must be current at the time of submission.

### 5. QMS Evidence — ISO 13485
ISO 13485 certificate from an internationally accredited certification body, covering the relevant product category.

### 6. Risk Management — ISO 14971
Risk management summary covering hazard identification, risk controls, and benefit-risk analysis.

### 7. Clinical / Performance Evaluation
- Class B: Clinical evaluation report (CER) or equivalent literature-based evidence summarising existing clinical data supporting safety and performance
- Class C: Detailed CER; may require clinical investigation data for novel devices
- Class D: Comprehensive clinical evidence; rigorous CER

## Separate Certificate Per Device

As a general rule, a **separate CMDR is required for each device**. Shared or grouped applications may be possible under specific conditions — confirm current shared application conditions with the CDRRHR before attempting a grouped submission.

## 90-Day Compliance Period for Deficiencies — PH-Unique

If CDRRHR identifies deficiencies in the application:
1. CDRRHR issues a deficiency notice
2. Applicant has **90 calendar days** to submit a complete response addressing all deficiencies
3. If the response is incomplete or not submitted within 90 days, the application is deemed **abandoned**
4. An abandoned application requires a fresh re-application with fees

**Strategic tip:** Prepare a complete, high-quality dossier to minimise deficiencies. If deficiencies are received, respond completely — partial responses restart the deficiency clock without resolving issues.

## Re-Application After Disapproval

If a CMDR application is formally disapproved (rejected), the applicant may re-apply within **60 days of the disapproval notice**. The re-application must address all grounds for disapproval. After 60 days, a fresh application with fees is required.

## CMDR Validity and Renewal

The CMDR has a defined validity period. Submit a renewal application before expiry with:
- Current LTO
- Updated ISO 13485 certificate
- Updated FSC
- Declaration of any changes during the previous validity period
