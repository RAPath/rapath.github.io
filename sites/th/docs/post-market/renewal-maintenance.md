# Renewal & Maintenance

> *5-year validity for all approval types · Renewal mandatory*

## 5-Year Approval Renewal

All Thai FDA product approvals (Listing, Notification, License) expire after **5 years** from the date of issue. Renewal is mandatory — there is no automatic extension.

### Renewal Process

1. Submit renewal application via Thai FDA e-submission system **at least 3–6 months before expiry**
2. Provide updated ISO 13485 certificate (must be current)
3. Confirm Establishment License is still valid
4. Declare any changes to the device during the 5-year period:
   - If significant changes occurred: a variation notification may be required first
5. Thai FDA reviews renewal application
6. Updated approval certificate issued for a further 5-year period

### Consequences of Lapsed Approval

A lapsed approval means the device **cannot legally be distributed or imported** in Thailand until renewal is achieved. Track expiry dates rigorously — set calendar reminders at 12 months, 6 months, and 3 months before expiry.

## Product Variation Notifications

During the 5-year approval period, notify Thai FDA of significant changes:

| Change Type | Notification Needed |
|------------|-------------------|
| Device design, materials, or specifications | ✅ Yes |
| Change in intended purpose or indication | ✅ Yes |
| Manufacturing site change | ✅ Yes |
| Significant labelling change | ✅ Yes |
| New model added to existing group | ✅ Yes (variation) |
| Administrative changes (address, company name) | ✅ Yes |
| Minor labelling changes (no safety impact) | May be a minor variation — confirm |

Submit a variation application with supporting evidence that the change does not compromise device safety or performance.

## Transfer of License Holder

If the Thai AR or license holder changes:
1. Submit a licence transfer application to Thai FDA
2. Both outgoing and incoming entities must cooperate
3. Incoming entity assumes all post-market obligations
4. Ensure Establishment License of incoming entity is valid before transfer
5. Plan the transfer to avoid any gap in valid product approval

## Cancellation of Registration

An approval may be voluntarily cancelled (product withdrawn from market) or cancelled by Thai FDA due to:
- Safety concerns
- Persistent non-compliance
- Failure to renew
- Submission of fraudulent documentation

Before cancelling a registration voluntarily, ensure all distributed devices are accounted for and consider whether any post-market actions (FSCAs) are needed.
