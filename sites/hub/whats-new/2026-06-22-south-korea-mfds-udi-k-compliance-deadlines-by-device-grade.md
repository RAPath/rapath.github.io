:::info Key Date
**2027-07-01** — UDI-K Compliance Deadlines by Device Grade — Unique Device Identification (Korea)
:::

MFDS is implementing **UDI-K** (Unique Device Identification — Korea) on a phased schedule by device grade:

- **Grade IV** (highest risk): Mandatory UDI-K — deadline passed (2022)
- **Grade III**: Mandatory UDI-K — deadline passed (2024)
- **Grade II**: Mandatory UDI-K — confirm current deadline with MFDS
- **Grade I**: Mandatory UDI-K — later implementation phase

UDI-K requires manufacturers to:
- Assign a UDI-DI and UDI-PI using an MFDS-recognised issuing agency.
- Label devices with the UDI in human-readable and machine-readable (barcode/GS1 DataMatrix) format.
- Register UDI data in the **KIMS** (Korea Integrated Medical Supplies) database.

Manufacturers with Grade I or II devices supplying the Korean market should confirm the current mandatory UDI-K compliance deadline for their device grade directly with MFDS or their Korean importer, as dates are updated periodically.
