The TGA is seeking public feedback on a proposal to publish Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) compliance inspection information on the TGA website. The purpose is to improve transparency of regulatory oversight of medical device manufacturing and related compliance outcomes.

📅 **Effective / published:** 2026-07-13

The tga is inviting written submissions from manufacturers, sponsors, importers, and other industry stakeholders. Review the draft guidance or proposal at the official source, prepare comments identifying any practical impacts on your operations or products, and submit before 2026-07-13. Active participation helps ensure the final guidance reflects industry realities and gives your organisation early visibility of upcoming requirements.
