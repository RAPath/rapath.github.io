<div className="update-meta"><span className="update-jurisdiction">🇮🇳 India (CDSCO)</span><span className="update-category">🔄 Reform Update</span></div>

CDSCO is rolling out India's Unique Device Identification (UDI) framework, aligned with the IMDRF UDI guidance. Under the framework, manufacturers are required to:

- Assign a **UDI-DI** (Device Identifier) and **UDI-PI** (Production Identifier) to each device and its packaging.
- Label devices with the UDI in human-readable and machine-readable format.
- Submit device information to the **UDID (Unique Device Identification Database)** maintained by CDSCO through the SUGAM portal.

🔖 **Status:** phased implementation; higher-risk device classes first

Manufacturers supplying devices in India should monitor CDSCO announcements for the applicable UDI implementation timeline for their device class. Early engagement with your IAR to coordinate SUGAM account setup and UDID submissions is recommended, as the portal requires Indian business credentials.
