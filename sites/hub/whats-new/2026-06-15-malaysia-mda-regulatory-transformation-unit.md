<div className="update-meta"><span className="update-jurisdiction">🇲🇾 Malaysia (MDA)</span><span className="update-category">🔄 Reform Update</span></div>

MDA has established a Regulatory Transformation Unit within its organizational structure to drive modernization and improvement of the medical device regulatory framework. This unit supports ongoing regulatory reform initiatives.

🔖 **Status:** in-development

This reform is part of the MDA's ongoing programme to modernise and strengthen the Malaysia medical device regulatory framework. Monitor the official source for updates on timelines, consultation opportunities, and implementation guidance. Where relevant, begin assessing potential impacts on your existing registrations, technical documentation, and compliance timelines so your organisation is prepared when the reform takes effect.
