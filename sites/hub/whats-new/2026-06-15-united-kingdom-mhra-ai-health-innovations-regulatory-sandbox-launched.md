The London sandbox is a secure environment designed to safely test AI-enabled medical devices in real-world settings. This initiative allows patients to benefit from innovative AI health technologies more quickly while maintaining safety standards.

🔖 **Status:** in-development

This reform is part of the MHRA's ongoing programme to modernise and strengthen the the UK medical device regulatory framework. Monitor the official source for updates on timelines, consultation opportunities, and implementation guidance.
