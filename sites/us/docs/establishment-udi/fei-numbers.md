# FEI Numbers & DUNS Numbers

## FDA Establishment Identifier (FEI)

When a facility registers with FDA, it is assigned an **FEI (FDA Establishment Identifier)** — a unique 10-digit number that identifies the registered facility. The FEI:

- Appears on establishment registration records
- Is referenced in FDA inspection records and warning letters
- Is used in 510(k) and PMA submissions to identify the manufacturing facility
- Is publicly searchable in FDA's establishment registration database

## DUNS Number

A **DUNS (Data Universal Numbering System)** number is a 9-digit identifier assigned by Dun & Bradstreet. Some FDA systems (including certain electronic submissions) request a DUNS number for establishment identification. A DUNS number can be obtained free of charge from Dun & Bradstreet.

## Finding your FEI

1. Go to [FDA's Establishment Registration and Device Listing database](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfrl/rl.cfm)
2. Search by establishment name, location, or FEI number
