# De Novo Timelines & FDA Interaction

## MDUFA VI performance goals

Under MDUFA VI, FDA's target for De Novo requests is:

| Milestone | Goal |
|-----------|------|
| Acceptance review | 15 business days |
| Total Time to Decision | 150 calendar days |
| Substantive interaction | 100 calendar days |

## Pre-submission meetings (Q-Submissions)

For De Novo, a **Q-submission (pre-submission meeting)** before filing is strongly recommended. FDA can provide feedback on:

- Whether De Novo is the appropriate pathway
- Proposed intended use and indications
- Performance testing requirements
- Clinical study design (if needed)
- Proposed special controls framework

Q-submissions are free and FDA's target response time is 90 days (or 60 days for a meeting request).
