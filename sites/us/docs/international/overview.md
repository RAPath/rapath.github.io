# USA in the Global Regulatory Landscape

## FDA's international role

- **IMDRF founding member** — the US was a founding participant of the International Medical Device Regulators Forum
- **Global benchmark** — FDA 510(k) clearance and PMA approval are used as reference points in reliance pathways of multiple countries including Australia, Singapore, and Canada, demonstrating FDA's influence in international regulatory harmonization
- **GMP mutual recognition** — FDA has MRAs for GMP inspections with EU, Canada, and others

## Quick comparison

| Jurisdiction | Body | Submission | Equivalent to |
|---|---|---|---|
| USA | FDA / CDRH | 510(k) / PMA / De Novo | — |
| Australia | TGA | ARTG inclusion | 510(k) / PMA |
| EU | Notified body | CE marking (MDR) | 510(k) / PMA |
| Canada | Health Canada | Device Licence | 510(k) / PMA |
| Singapore | HSA | Product registration | 510(k) / PMA |
