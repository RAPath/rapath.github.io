# Reforms Tracker

A consolidated quick-reference for regulatory reforms affecting Australian medical device sponsors and manufacturers. Always verify reform status and dates against official TGA sources.

👉 [What's new for medical devices — TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/overview/changes-medical-device-regulations)

---

## Currently In Effect

| Reform | Effective Date | Key Impact | Source |
|--------|---------------|-----------|--------|
| **Mandatory healthcare facility reporting — high-risk** | 21 March 2026 | Hospitals must report adverse events involving Class III, AIMD, IVD Class 4 | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/reporting-medical-device-adverse-events-healthcare-facilities) |
| **New TGA AI / SaMD guidance** | February 2026 | Clarifies AI regulated under existing SaMD framework; no separate AI regime | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/software-and-artificial-intelligence-ai) |
| **TGA Compliance Principles 2026–2027** | January 2026 | Priority focus: DTC IVDs, fetal dopplers, SaMD, cosmetic procedure devices | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/news/media-releases/tga-releases-compliance-principles-reinforcing-proactive-and-risk-based-enforcement-throughout-2026-and-2027) |
| **PRAC replaces URPTG** | 5 March 2025 | New recall procedure — 4 market action types, 5-step process | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/safety/recalls-and-other-market-actions/procedure-recalls-product-alerts-and-product-corrections-prac) |
| **Healthcare facility reporting — voluntary period** | 21 March 2025 | 12-month voluntary period (now ended); mandatory from 21 March 2026 | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/reporting-medical-device-adverse-events-healthcare-facilities) |
| **Patient implant cards mandatory** | 1 November 2024 | Required for all implantable devices supplied in Australia | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/labelling-and-advertising/patient-information-materials) |
| **SaMD ARTG inclusion mandatory** | 1 November 2024 | All software medical devices must be in ARTG or covered by exemption | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/software-and-artificial-intelligence-ai) |
| **New SaMD classification rules** | 1 November 2024 | Software classified by severity of harm from incorrect output | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/classifying-active-medical-devices-australia-including-software-based-medical-devices) |
| **AIMD reclassification deadline** | 1 November 2024 | All AIMD entries must have transitioned to Class III or ceased supply | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/reclassifying-active-implantable-medical-devices-aimd) |
| **Regulation 5.3 updated** | 1 July 2024 | Mandatory audit for Class III + MDD/510(k); EU MDR certificates preferred | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/using-assessments-comparable-overseas-regulators-medical-devices) |
| **Animal/microbial/recombinant devices** | 1 July 2024 | Rule 5.5 now applies to animal origin only (not microbial/recombinant) | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/medical-devices-containing-medicinal-microbial-recombinant-or-animal-origin-substances) |
| **Australian CABs operational** | 2022 | Domestic conformity assessment body pathway available | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/manufacturing/obtain-and-maintain-regulatory-evidence) |
| **EU IVDR certificates accepted** | 28 July 2021 | IVDR notified body certificates accepted for IVD ARTG applications | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/using-assessments-comparable-overseas-regulators-medical-devices) |
| **AIMD → Class III commenced** | 25 November 2021 | New active implantable applications classified as Class III | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/reclassifying-active-implantable-medical-devices-aimd) |

## Currently in Transition

| Reform | Current Status | Key Date | Action Required |
|--------|---------------|---------|----------------|
