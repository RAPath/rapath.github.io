# Reforms Tracker

A consolidated quick-reference for regulatory reforms affecting Australian medical device sponsors and manufacturers. Always verify reform status and dates against official TGA sources.

👉 [What's new for medical devices — TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/overview/changes-medical-device-regulations)

---

## Currently In Effect

| Reform | Effective Date | Key Impact | Source |
|--------|---------------|-----------|--------|
| PRAC replaces URPTG | 5 March 2025 | New recall procedure — 4 market action types, 5-step process | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/safety/recalls-and-other-market-actions/procedure-recalls-product-alerts-and-product-corrections-prac) |
| Healthcare facility reporting — voluntary period | 21 March 2025 | Hospitals can report via ASDER; mandatory from March 2026 | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/reporting-medical-device-adverse-events-healthcare-facilities) |
| Patient implant cards mandatory | 1 November 2024 | Required for all implantable devices supplied in Australia | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/labelling-and-advertising/patient-information-materials) |
| SaMD ARTG inclusion mandatory | 1 November 2024 | All software medical devices must be in ARTG or covered by exemption | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/software-and-artificial-intelligence-ai) |
| New SaMD classification rules | 1 November 2024 | Software classified by severity of harm (Class IIa / IIb / III) | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/classifying-active-medical-devices-australia-including-software-based-medical-devices) |
| AIMD reclassification deadline | 1 November 2024 | All AIMD entries must have transitioned to Class III or ceased supply | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/reclassifying-active-implantable-medical-devices-aimd) |
| Regulation 5.3 updated | 1 July 2024 | Mandatory audit for Class III + MDD/510(k); EU MDR certificates preferred | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/using-assessments-comparable-overseas-regulators-medical-devices) |
| Animal/microbial/recombinant devices | 1 July 2024 | New classification and CA requirements for affected device types | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/medical-devices-containing-medicinal-microbial-recombinant-or-animal-origin-substances) |
| Australian CABs operational | 2022 | Domestic conformity assessment body pathway available | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/products/medical-devices/manufacturing/obtain-and-maintain-regulatory-evidence) |
| EU IVDR certificates accepted | 28 July 2021 | IVDR notified body certificates accepted for IVD ARTG applications | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/using-assessments-comparable-overseas-regulators-medical-devices) |
| AIMD → Class III reclassification commenced | 25 November 2021 | New active implantable applications classified as Class III | [TGA](https://www.tga.gov.au/resources/guidance/reclassifying-active-implantable-medical-devices-aimd) |

## Currently in Transition

| Reform | Current Status | Key Date | Action Required |
|--------|---------------|---------|----------------|
| Healthcare facility mandatory reporting | Voluntary period | **21 March 2026** — mandatory for high-risk devices | Facilities: register for ASDER; Sponsors: update vigilance processes |
| EU MDR transition | MDD certificates still accepted (with mandatory audit) | MDD certificates progressively expiring | Plan MDR transition with manufacturer; submit ARTG variations as needed |
| EU IVDR transition | IVDD and IVDR certificates accepted | EU IVDR transition ongoing | Monitor EU notified body IVDR certifications |
| UDI implementation | Higher-risk classes already required; others phasing in | Progressive rollout ongoing | Check TGA UDI hub for your device class requirement date |

## Upcoming — Confirmed

| Reform | Expected Date | Impact | Action |
|--------|--------------|--------|--------|
| Mandatory reporting — high-risk devices | **21 March 2026** | Mandatory reporting by healthcare facilities for Class III, IVD Class 4 | Healthcare facilities and sponsors of high-risk devices |
| Mandatory reporting — medium-risk + near misses | **1 April 2028** | Expanded mandatory reporting scope | Sponsors of Class IIa, IIb, IVD Class 2, IVD Class 3 |
| Further reporting expansion | **1 April 2030** | Additional event types | Monitor TGA guidance |
