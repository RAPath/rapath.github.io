# Active Devices & INMETRO Certification

:::info BR-unique
Certain active (electrically powered) medical devices require **compulsory INMETRO certification** in addition to ANVISA registration — a dual-approval requirement unique to Brazil.
:::

INMETRO certification is required for electrical and electronic medical equipment listed in INMETRO's compulsory certification *portaria*. Check the current list before finalising your regulatory strategy.

**INMETRO certification process:** Engage an INMETRO-accredited Certification Body (OCP) → Submit for testing against ABNT NBR IEC 60601 standards → Factory audit (if required) → OCP issues certificate → Apply INMETRO conformity mark.

- [INMETRO — Avaliação da Conformidade](https://www.gov.br/inmetro/pt-br/assuntos/avaliacao-da-conformidade)
