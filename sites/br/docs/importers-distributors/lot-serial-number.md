# Lot/Serial Number Requirements

All medical devices placed on the Brazilian market must have clearly visible **lot/batch numbers** (*número do lote*) on the outer packaging and, where space permits, on the device itself.

**Serial numbers** (unique per individual unit) are required for:
- Implantable devices (to enable patient-level traceability); and
- Active devices where individual unit tracking is needed for safety monitoring.

Lot/batch numbers and serial numbers must be:
- Permanent and legible;
- Placed in a location that will not be obscured or damaged in normal use; and
- Preceded by the word "Lote:" or the batch symbol.

These markings enable ANVISA and healthcare facilities to trace devices involved in adverse events or recalls back to specific production batches and distribution chains.
