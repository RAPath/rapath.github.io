# SNVS — National Health Surveillance System

:::info BR-unique
The SNVS (*Sistema Nacional de Vigilância Sanitária*) is a uniquely Brazilian coordinated system integrating federal, state and municipal health surveillance. No direct parallel exists in AU, EU, US, SG, or CA frameworks.
:::

## How SNVS operates for medical devices

| Level | Body | Key activities |
|---|---|---|
| Federal | ANVISA | National policy, product registration, AFE grants, import surveillance at ports of entry |
| State | VISA estadual | State GMP inspections, regional market surveillance |
| Municipal | VISA municipal | Local operating licences, inspections of distribution and retail premises |

## NOTIVISA — the surveillance reporting hub

**NOTIVISA** is the national adverse event and technical complaint reporting system. Manufacturers, importers, distributors, and healthcare facilities are obligated to report adverse events via NOTIVISA. See [NOTIVISA](/docs/post-market/adverse-events/notivisa) for details.
