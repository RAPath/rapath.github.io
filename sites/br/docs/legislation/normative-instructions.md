# Normative Instructions & Portarias

In addition to RDCs (*Resoluções da Diretoria Colegiada*), ANVISA issues:

**Instruções Normativas (INs)** — normative instructions that provide detailed operational requirements within the framework set by an RDC. Key medical device INs include:
- **IN ANVISA 57/2022** — Classification catalogue for medical devices
- **IN ANVISA 77/2021** — Classification catalogue updates

**Portarias** — ministerial orders issued by the Ministry of Health or ANVISA that may contain specific technical requirements or fee tables.

**Notas Técnicas** — technical notes issued by ANVISA providing guidance on specific topics (not binding, but ANVISA's official interpretation).

All ANVISA regulations are published in the **Diário Oficial da União (DOU)** and available on the ANVISA website.

- [DOU — Diário Oficial da União](https://www.in.gov.br)
- [ANVISA legislation portal](https://www.gov.br/anvisa/pt-br/assuntos/regulamentacao/resolucoes)

---

:::note Official sources
Verify all information against official [ANVISA](https://www.gov.br/anvisa) sources before making regulatory decisions.
:::

These INs support the core RDC 751/2022 framework and its companion regulations (RDC 752–755/2022). IN 57/2022 provides the detailed classification catalogue referenced in RDC 751 Annex I.
