# ABNT Standards Recognised by ANVISA

**ABNT** (*Associação Brasileira de Normas Técnicas*) is Brazil's national standards body. ABNT issues technical standards (*Normas ABNT NBR*) that are adaptations of international standards (ISO, IEC) or original Brazilian standards.

ANVISA recognises ABNT standards as means of demonstrating compliance with the Essential Requirements in RDC 751/2022. Key harmonised ABNT standards for medical devices include:

| ABNT Standard | International Equivalent | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| ABNT NBR ISO 14971 | ISO 14971:2019 | Risk management |
| ABNT NBR ISO 13485 | ISO 13485:2016 | Quality management systems |
| ABNT NBR IEC 60601-1 | IEC 60601-1 | General requirements for electrical medical devices |
| ABNT NBR IEC 60601-1-2 | IEC 60601-1-2 | EMC requirements |
| ABNT NBR IEC 62304 | IEC 62304 | Software lifecycle |
| ABNT NBR IEC 62366-1 | IEC 62366-1 | Usability engineering |
| ABNT NBR ISO 10993-1 | ISO 10993-1 | Biocompatibility evaluation |
| ABNT NBR ISO 11135 | ISO 11135 | Sterilisation by EtO |
