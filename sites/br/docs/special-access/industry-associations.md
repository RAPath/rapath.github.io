# Industry Associations — SBCTA / ABIMO

Several industry associations represent the medical device and IVD sector in Brazil, engaging with ANVISA on regulatory matters and providing resources for industry.

## Key associations

### ABIMO — Associação Brasileira da Indústria de Artigos e Equipamentos Médicos e Odontológicos

ABIMO is the primary trade association for Brazilian medical device and dental equipment manufacturers. Key activities:
- Participates in ANVISA public consultations on draft regulations;
- Provides regulatory training and guidance for member companies;
- Represents Brazilian industry in IMDRF, ISO, and MERCOSUL technical committees; and
- Publishes regulatory updates and guidance for members.

### SBCTA — Sociedade Brasileira de Ciência e Tecnologia de Alimentos / health tech associations

Various other sector-specific associations represent IVD manufacturers, radiation equipment companies, and healthcare technology providers.

### SINDIMED — Sindicato Nacional da Indústria de Produtos para Saúde

SINDIMED represents the interests of the medical device industry in labour and industrial policy matters.

## International associations active in Brazil

- **EUCOMED / MedTech Europe** — represents European manufacturers in Brazil
- **AdvaMed** — US medical device industry association with Brazil engagement
- **JFMDA** — Japan Federation of Medical Devices Associations — active in MERCOSUL regulatory harmonisation
