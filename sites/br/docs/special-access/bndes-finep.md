# BNDES / FINEP — Domestic Manufacturing Incentives

:::info BR-unique
Brazil actively incentivises domestic medical device manufacturing through financing programmes offered by **BNDES** (Banco Nacional de Desenvolvimento Econômico e Social) and **FINEP** (Financiadora de Estudos e Projetos).
:::

## BNDES — Development bank financing

BNDES offers financing for medical device manufacturers in Brazil through:

- **BNDES Finame** — financing for the acquisition of nationally manufactured equipment (including medical devices) by healthcare facilities;
- **BNDES Finem** — long-term financing for investment in production capacity, GMP upgrades, and R&D facilities; and
- **BNDES Inovação** — innovation financing for development of new medical technologies.

## FINEP — Innovation funding

FINEP provides grants and soft loans for medical device R&D and clinical development:

- Non-refundable grants (*subvenção econômica*) for innovative medical device development by Brazilian companies;
- Soft loans for clinical trials and regulatory development; and
- Support for technology transfer and scale-up.

## EMBRAPII and research partnerships

**EMBRAPII** (*Empresa Brasileira de Pesquisa e Inovação Industrial*) facilitates partnerships between Brazilian manufacturers and research institutes for collaborative medical device R&D.
