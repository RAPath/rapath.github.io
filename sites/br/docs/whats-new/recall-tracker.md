# ANVISA Recall & Alert Tracker

ANVISA publishes official **recall notices** (*comunicados de recolhimento*) and **safety alerts** (*alertas de segurança*) for medical devices on its website. This page explains how to access and monitor this information.

## Finding ANVISA recalls and safety alerts

ANVISA publishes recall and safety alert information in two locations:

1. **ANVISA website** — the primary source for official communications:
   - [ANVISA — Recalls and Alerts](https://www.gov.br/anvisa/pt-br/assuntos/produtossaude/produtos-para-saude/tecnovigilancia/alertas-e-recalls)

2. **Diário Oficial da União (DOU)** — compulsory recalls and regulatory actions are published in the DOU:
   - [DOU](https://www.in.gov.br)

## Types of ANVISA safety communications

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Comunicado de Recolhimento | Official recall notice — voluntary or compulsory |
| Alerta de Segurança | Safety alert — advises healthcare professionals and patients of a safety issue |
| Nota Técnica de Segurança | Technical safety note — provides detailed guidance on a safety issue |
| Medida Cautelar | Emergency precautionary measure — immediate action required |

## Monitoring recalls as part of PMS

Registration holders and distributors must monitor ANVISA's recall and safety alert database as part of their post-market surveillance (PMS) programme — both for their own devices and for competitor devices where safety signals may be relevant.

- [ANVISA — Tecnovigilância alerts](https://www.gov.br/anvisa/pt-br/assuntos/produtossaude/produtos-para-saude/tecnovigilancia)
