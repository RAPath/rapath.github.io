# IVD Post-Market Performance Follow-Up (PMPF)

For IVDs, the equivalent of PMCF is **Post-Market Performance Follow-Up (PMPF)**, which involves the proactive collection of post-market performance data to confirm the IVD's analytical and clinical performance in routine use.

PMPF is required for Class III and IV IVDs and should include:
- Monitoring of ongoing laboratory proficiency testing data;
- Review of published literature on the IVD or equivalent devices;
- Active follow-up with key clinical laboratory users; and
- Periodic reanalysis of clinical performance data from the registration dossier against real-world performance.
